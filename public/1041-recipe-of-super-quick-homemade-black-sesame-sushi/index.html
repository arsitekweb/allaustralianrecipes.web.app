The ingredients needed to make Black Sesame Sushi:
  1. Prepare 1 parsnip
  2. Make ready 2 nori sheets
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup pea shoots
  4. Get 1/4 cup cucumber
  5. Take 1/4 cup red pepper
  6. Make ready 1/2 avocado
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  8. Take 1 tsp wasabi
  9. Make ready 1 tsp fresh ginger
  10. Get 1 tbsp fresh coriander leaf
  11. Get 1 tbsp black sesame seeds
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp mirin
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp rice vinegar
