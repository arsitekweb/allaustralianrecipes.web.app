The ingredients needed to make Curried Parsnip Soup:
  1. Make ready 5 medium size Parsnips
  2. Take 1 Maris Piper potato
  3. Make ready 1 medium size onion
  4. Take 2 cloves garlic
  5. Get 1 tsp cumin
  6. Take 1 tsp thyme
  7. Take 2 tsp mild curry powder
  8. Prepare 2 tablespoons creme fraiche
  9. Make ready Chicken stock gel or cube of choice

Instructions to make Curried Parsnip Soup:
  1. Chop all veg. Sweat off onion in olive oil over medium heat till soft. Add garlic,thyme & powder.
  2. Chop parsnips & potato & cook for a further 5-10 mins
  3. Add enough hot water to cover veg. Add stock & cook till all veg is tender
  4. Blend add creme fraiche & season
