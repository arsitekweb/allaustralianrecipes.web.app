Instructions to make Deep Fried Parsnip Peels:
  1. Pre-wash the parsnips before peeling.
  2. Line a bowl with kitchen roll and peel the parsnips into the bowl as you go along.
  3. Heat up the fryer and toss the peels into the fryer. Don't over load it, cook in batches if needed. Fry for about a minute or until the parsnips are golden brown and crispy.
  4. Line a dish with kitchen roll again and toss in the cooked parsnip peels.
  5. Season with salt and enjoy.
