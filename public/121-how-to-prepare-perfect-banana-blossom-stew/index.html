The ingredients needed to make Banana Blossom Stew:
  1. Prepare 1 tin Banana blossom drained
  2. Get 3 carrots
  3. Take 3 celery stalks
  4. Get 2 onions
  5. Get 2 parsnips
  6. Prepare 1 can plum tomatoes
  7. Make ready 1 veggie stock cube
  8. Prepare Tablespoon flour
  9. Take 2 rosemary sprigs
  10. Prepare 250 ml red wine

Steps to make Banana Blossom Stew:
  1. Cut all the veg into chunky pieces and fry in a casserole dish for approx 10 mins in oil.
  2. Add flour and rosemary
  3. Stir in wine and leave for 2 mins for wine to thicken
  4. Add tomatoes, fill can with water and put in. Add crushed stock cube and Banana Blossom.
  5. Place in oven @180°c for 90 mins.
