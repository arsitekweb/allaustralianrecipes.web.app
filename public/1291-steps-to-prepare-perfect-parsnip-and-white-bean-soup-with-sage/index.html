The ingredients needed to make Parsnip and white bean soup with sage:
  1. Get 500 g parsnips peeled and chopped
  2. Take 1 small white onion chopped
  3. Get 1 tin Canelloni beans
  4. Make ready 1 vegetable stock pot
  5. Take 1 1/2 pints water
  6. Take 4 sage leaves
  7. Get to taste salt and white pepper
