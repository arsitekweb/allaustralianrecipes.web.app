The ingredients needed to make My Deep Fried Parsnip Crisp:
  1. Prepare 2 whole parsnips, peeled and ends chopped
  2. Take 1 Sea Salt
  3. Make ready 1 pints Veg oil

Steps to make My Deep Fried Parsnip Crisp:
  1. Peel parsnips
  2. Run them under cold tap water and dry.
  3. With the potato peeler, dig in to get a thicker shavings of parsnip
  4. Heat the veg oil in a deep fryer, until really hot
  5. Add a few at a time in the deep fryer quick shake, and fry for about 3-4 minutes
  6. When done shake off excess oil and put on kitchen roll paper to drain
  7. Add a good helping of Sea Salt and add to serving dish
  8. You could add black pepper or a little paprika to them or lemon pepper is good also

As soon as they are ready, remove the parsnip crisps with a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen paper. Immediately season generously with the sea.
