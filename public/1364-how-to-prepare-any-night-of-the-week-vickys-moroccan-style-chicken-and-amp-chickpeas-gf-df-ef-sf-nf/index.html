Instructions to make Vickys Moroccan-Style Chicken & Chickpeas, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Preheat the oven to gas 7 / 220C / 450°F
  2. Put the chicken and vegetables in a roasting tray, drizzle with the oil and toss together
  3. Sprinkle over the seasonings and toss again, then place the chicken on top roast for 40 minutes - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/358307-vickys-ras-el-hanout-moroccan-spice-mix-gluten-dairy-egg-soy-free
  4. Remove the chicken and let rest on a warm plate covered in foil while you finish the vegetables off
  5. Put the roasting tin with the vegetables on the stove top over a medium heat and add in the chickpeas and tomatoes
  6. Let simmer for a few minutes to thicken then plate up and place the chicken legs on top
  7. Serve with cous cous and warm flatbreads to stretch the meal a bit further
