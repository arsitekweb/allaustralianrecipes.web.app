Instructions to make Irmgards iffits bung it all in Soup very healthy and low calorie:
  1. Add all veg to a medium sized pan
  2. Add water
  3. Crumble on stock cube & sprinkle all spices on.
  4. Stir, then pop lid on and leave until veg are soft. I used an 8 in 1 on 140 for 45 minutes
  5. Cool for few minutes, Stir then blend with a stick blender until smooth.
  6. Divide into 4 bowls..I got 1350 mils total of 250 calories. Serve or put into lidded containers & keep in the fridge for upto 4 days.
