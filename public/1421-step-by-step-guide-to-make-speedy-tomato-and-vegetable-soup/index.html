The ingredients needed to make Tomato and vegetable soup:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cauliflower
  2. Make ready 1 carrot
  3. Get 1/2 parsnip
  4. Get 1/2 can chopped tomato
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp tomato paste
  6. Make ready 1/2 onion
  7. Make ready 1 clove garlic
  8. Prepare 500 ml veg stock
  9. Get Basil
  10. Get Oregano
  11. Prepare Salt
  12. Make ready Pepper
  13. Make ready Chilli (optional)
