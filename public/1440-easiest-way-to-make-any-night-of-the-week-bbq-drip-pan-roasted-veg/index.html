Steps to make BBQ Drip pan roasted veg:
  1. Choose your selection of veg and cut into equal sizes.
  2. Add to a bowl and season with olive oil, mixed herbs and salt and pepper.
  3. Add to a drip pan and place this under the meat you are cooking on the bbq. The juices collect in the pan from the meat and in turn add a delicious flavour to the veg.
  4. Roast at around 200c for 45 minutes or adjust the time and temprature to the meat you are cooking.
