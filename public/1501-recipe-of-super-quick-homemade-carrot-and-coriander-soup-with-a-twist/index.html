The ingredients needed to make Carrot and coriander soup with a twist:
  1. Make ready 570 grams carrots ends chopped, peel on optional and sliced
  2. Make ready 181 grams parsnips ends chopped, peel left on optional and sliced
  3. Prepare 95 grams peeled and cubed potatoes
  4. Take 1 vegetable stock cube
  5. Make ready 900 ml Cold Water
  6. Get 1 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  7. Take 1 tsp asafoetida
