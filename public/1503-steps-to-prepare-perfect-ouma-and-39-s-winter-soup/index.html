The ingredients needed to make Ouma's winter soup:
  1. Get 1.5 kg beef on bone
  2. Get Soup vegetables (suede, turnip, carrots, parsnip)
  3. Get 3 stalks celery, sliced
  4. Take 1 tbsp peppercorns
  5. Prepare 1 bunch red horseradish
  6. Make ready 2 leeks, sliced
  7. Get 2 sprigs thyme
  8. Make ready 2 sprigs rosemary
  9. Take 2 bay leaf
  10. Prepare 1 handful barley
  11. Take 1 handful spaghetti, broken into 1inch pieces
  12. Get 1 bunch parsley, chopped
  13. Prepare 2 spring onion, chopped
  14. Take 1-2 litres beef stock
  15. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
