The ingredients needed to make Andry's Parsnip Puree:
  1. Get 400 grams Potatoes
  2. Prepare 1 Parsnip
  3. Prepare 100 ml Milk
  4. Take 10 grams Butter
  5. Make ready 1 pinch Salt
  6. Make ready 1 dash Grounded nutmeg

Instructions to make Andry's Parsnip Puree:
  1. Peel the potatoes and parsnip into cubes
  2. Boil them together in water for 15-18 minutes with a pinch of salt
  3. Drain them and ash them together
  4. Add the preheated milk and mix them together with the butter and nutmeg
