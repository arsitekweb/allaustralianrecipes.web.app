Steps to make Pork joint cooked in milk:
  1. Wash and dry meat and leave at room temperature for an hour. Finely chop some carrots, onions and celery. Fry gently in oil for a few mins
  2. Add meat browning on both sides for a further 2-3 mins. Add wine and turn up the heat for 1 min. Add salt and pepper
  3. Add milk until the meat is nearly covered. Bring gently to the boil, cover and then simmer on lowest flame for about 2 hours. Turn a few times during cooking
