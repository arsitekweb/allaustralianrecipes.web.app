The ingredients needed to make Veggie blondies:
  1. Get 4 parsnips
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cup flour
  3. Make ready 2 sugar in the raw
  4. Make ready 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  5. Get 1 evaporated milk
  6. Take 1 vanilla extract
  7. Get 1 cinnamon

Instructions to make Veggie blondies:

  2. Boil parsnips until soft
  3. Mix parsnips with milk
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Fold in vanilla and carrot powder
