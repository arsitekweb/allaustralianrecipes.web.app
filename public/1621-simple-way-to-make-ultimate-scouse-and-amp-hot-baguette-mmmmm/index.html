The ingredients needed to make Scouse & hot baguette Mmmmm…:
  1. Get 1 lb stewing steak
  2. Prepare 1 red onion
  3. Prepare 1 leek
  4. Take 3 carrots
  5. Get 2 parsnips
  6. Take 7 large potatoes
  7. Prepare 7 large cabbage leaves, i prefer the greener ones.
  8. Take 4 chicken stock cubes
  9. Take 3 tbsp gravy
  10. Take 1 tbsp tomato puree
