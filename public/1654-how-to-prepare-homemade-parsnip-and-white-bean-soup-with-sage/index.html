The ingredients needed to make Parsnip and white bean soup with sage:
  1. Prepare 500 g parsnips peeled and chopped
  2. Take 1 small white onion chopped
  3. Make ready 1 tin Canelloni beans
  4. Prepare 1 vegetable stock pot
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 pints water
  6. Prepare 4 sage leaves
  7. Prepare to taste salt and white pepper

Steps to make Parsnip and white bean soup with sage:
  1. Lightly fry onions in a large saucepan
  2. Add parsnips and cook for 2 minutes
  3. Add beans, stock and water and sage
  4. Cook for 20 minutes then blend until smooth
