The ingredients needed to make Hearty Vegetable Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  2. Make ready 1 onion
  3. Make ready 2 large potatoes
  4. Get 2 large carrots
  5. Make ready 1 parsnip
  6. Make ready 1.5 litres vegetable stock (2 stock cubes)
  7. Get 4 stalks celery
