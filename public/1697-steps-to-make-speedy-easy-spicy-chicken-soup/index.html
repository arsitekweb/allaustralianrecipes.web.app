The ingredients needed to make Easy Spicy Chicken Soup:
  1. Prepare 3 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  2. Prepare 1 large onion
  3. Prepare 3 large carrots
  4. Get 3 piece celery
  5. Get 3 small parsnips
  6. Take 2 jalapeños
  7. Take 1 tbsp Italian Seasoning
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp dried cilantro
  9. Prepare pepper, salt and granulated garlic
  10. Take 8 cup plus or minus chicken stock
  11. Take 20 oz pre cooked chicken meat
  12. Prepare 3 small Mexican summer squash

Instructions to make Easy Spicy Chicken Soup:
  1. Chop and dice parsnips, carrots, celery, onion and jalapeños. Add them to a pot with olive oil.
  2. Add seasoning and sauté until half cooked.
  3. While the vegetables are cooking chop and dice, the chicken and Summer squash.
  4. Add 6 cups of stock, the Summer squash and chicken. If you need more broth add what you need.
  5. Heat until hot.
