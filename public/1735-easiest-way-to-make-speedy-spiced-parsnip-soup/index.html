The ingredients needed to make Spiced parsnip soup:
  1. Prepare 3 cloves garlic
  2. Prepare 1 small onion
  3. Make ready 4 parsnips
  4. Take 3 carrots
  5. Take 1 potato
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon whole cumin
  7. Take 1 teaspoon turmeric
  8. Get 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes (adjust to taste)
  9. Take 500 ml stock
  10. Get 3 handfuls leafy greens (I used broad bean tops)
  11. Prepare 400 ml coconut milk

Steps to make Spiced parsnip soup:
  1. Rough chop the vegetables.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they start to pop add the garlic, onions and turmeric.
  3. Cook the onions for a few minutes before adding the rest of the vegetables and stock.
  4. Put a lid on the pan and leave it to boil until the vegetables are cooked.
  5. Mash the vegetables with a potato masher. If you prefer a smoother soup you could blend it here.
  6. Add the leafy greens and coconut milk and continue cooking.
  7. Enjoy! Maybe even with a sandwich.
