  1. Make ready 150 g spicy chorizo, sliced
  2. Prepare 250 g fresh mushrooms, sliced
  3. Make ready 2 large carrots, sliced
  4. Make ready 2 parsnips, sliced
  5. Take 1 stick celery cut up to small pieces
  6. Get 1 onion, finely chopped
  7. Get 1 tsp paprika
  8. Get to taste salt, pepper

  10. Take 2-3 tbs oil

  12. Get 1 tbsp soured cream (optional)
