The ingredients needed to make Parsnip Fries:
  1. Make ready 2-3 parsnips (about 250 g)
  2. Take Oil for deep frying
  3. Prepare 1 sprig rosemary (optional)
  4. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make Parsnip Fries:
  1. I used one big parsnip and two small ones. Scrub the skin clean. I don’t peel mine but you can if you want.
  2. Cut into 1 cm sticks or wedges.
  3. Heat oil (180C/350F if you’re measuring) and put in the parsnips and rosemary. It should be hot enough that it bubbles when you put in the parsnips.
  4. Fry until the parsnips have a little bit of color.
  5. Remove and drain over paper towels (fry in batches if needed).
  6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and pieces of the fried rosemary.
