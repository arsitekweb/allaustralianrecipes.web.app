The ingredients needed to make Black Sesame Sushi:
  1. Get 1 parsnip
  2. Get 2 nori sheets
  3. Take 1/2 cup pea shoots

  5. Make ready 1/4 cup red pepper
  6. Get 1/2 avocado
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  8. Make ready 1 tsp wasabi
  9. Make ready 1 tsp fresh ginger
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp fresh coriander leaf
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp black sesame seeds
  12. Take 2 tbsp mirin
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp rice vinegar
