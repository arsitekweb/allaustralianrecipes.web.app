The ingredients needed to make Ouma's winter soup:
  1. Get 1.5 kg beef on bone
  2. Get Soup vegetables (suede, turnip, carrots, parsnip)
  3. Prepare 3 stalks celery, sliced
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp peppercorns
  5. Take 1 bunch red horseradish
  6. Prepare 2 leeks, sliced
  7. Take 2 sprigs thyme
  8. Prepare 2 sprigs rosemary

  10. Prepare 1 handful barley
  11. Make ready 1 handful spaghetti, broken into 1inch pieces
