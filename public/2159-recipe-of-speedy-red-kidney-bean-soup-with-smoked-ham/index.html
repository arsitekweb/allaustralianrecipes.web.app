The ingredients needed to make Red kidney bean soup with smoked ham:
  1. Prepare 400 g red kidney beans
  2. Take 300 g smoked ham, cut to small pieces
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp oil
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp flour
  5. Make ready 1 tsp paprika powder
  6. Take 1 small onion
  7. Get 2 cloves garlic
  8. Take 2 large carrots
  9. Prepare 2 large parsnips
  10. Take salt (bear in mind the ham is already salted)
  11. Get pepper
  12. Make ready chilli (optional)
  13. Prepare sour cream (optional)
  14. Get For the pasta:
  15. Get 1 egg
  16. Make ready flour
  17. Make ready pinch salt
  18. Prepare pinch turmeric
