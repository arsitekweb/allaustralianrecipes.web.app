The ingredients needed to make Roasted Vegetable Sticks:
  1. Make ready 1 small beet
  2. Take 1 medium carrot
  3. Prepare 1 small parsnip
  4. Prepare 1 small potato
  5. Take 1/2 red or yellow bell pepper
  6. Prepare 1 small sweet potato
  7. Prepare 1 tsp ground cumin
  8. Get 3 tbsp olive oil

Instructions to make Roasted Vegetable Sticks:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Peel all vegetables except bell peppers
  3. Cut all except parsnip and sweet potato into size of thick fries, about 2 inches long
  4. Make the parsnip and sweet potato slightly larger
  5. Wash bell peppers well, deseed them and cut into strips about 1/2in wide
  6. Mix cumin and olive oil together
  7. Brush some oil onto baking sheet and heat in oven 2-3 min
  8. Place veggie sticks on baking sheet and brush all over with olive oil mixture
  9. Bake for 20-30 min until tender
