Steps to make Honey Glazed Carrots & Parsnips:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200ºC.
  2. Peel and slice the carrots and parsnips into roughly equally sized pieces.
  3. Mix together the oil, honey and salt in a large bowl.
  4. Add the sliced carrots and parsnips to the bowl, and mix together in the bowl until they're evenly coated.
  5. Place the vegetables on a baking tray, spreading them as evenly as possible.
  6. Roast for 45 minutes, or until the edges are nicely browned.
