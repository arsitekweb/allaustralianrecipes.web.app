The ingredients needed to make Curried parsnip soup:
  1. Prepare 1 onion
  2. Take 3 parsnips
  3. Prepare 850 ml water
  4. Make ready 1 stock cube
  5. Get 2 tsp garlic puree
  6. Take 1 lemon juice and zested
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp flour (plain or cornflower)
  8. Get 1 tbsp curry powder
  9. Take 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
  11. Take Salt and pepper to season

Instructions to make Curried parsnip soup:
  1. Dice onion and parsnips. Add to frying pan with oil and cook for 5-10 minutes until veg have softened. Add garlic and cook 1 further minute.
  2. Add curry powder and chilli and stir on heat. Add flour and stir well. Cook 30 seconds.
  3. Crumble in stock cube, lemon rind and juice and slowly add stock while stirring.
  4. Boil for 5 minutes then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Cool soup before blending until smooth. Then reheat to desired temperature and serve with bread or croutons.
