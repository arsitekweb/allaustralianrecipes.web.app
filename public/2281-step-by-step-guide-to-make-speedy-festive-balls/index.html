The ingredients needed to make Festive balls:
  1. Prepare 400 g parsnips
  2. Take 400 g Brussels sprouts
  3. Get 1 leek
  4. Make ready 80 g cashew nuts
  5. Prepare 1 block vegan mozzarella alternative
  6. Prepare Salt
  7. Make ready Pepper
  8. Take Sage

Make ahead of time to allow flavors to blend. In a meduim bowl, blend cheeses together with a fork. Mix in remaining ingredients except the walnuts and crackers. Meanwhile, combine sauce ingredients in a small saucepan; whisk over medium heat until heated through.

Instructions to make Festive balls:
  1. Trim and peel the parsnips and sprouts and add to a pan of boiling water until the parsnips are soft.
  2. Heat some oil in a pan and add the chopped leek. Drain the parsnips and sprouts and add those too.
  3. Season with salt and pepper. I added some sage too. Then, with a potato masher, mash the ingredients together. I ground up some cashews to help bind the mix.
  4. Using your hands create balls from the mixture. I cut up small cubes of vegan mozzarella and put those in the middle.
  5. Add to preheated (and oiled) baking tray and cook at 200°C for 30 mins, carefully turning once.
