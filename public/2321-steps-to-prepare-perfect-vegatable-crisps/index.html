Steps to make Vegatable Crisps:
  1. Pre heat oven to 160/140 fan or gas mark 3.
  2. Thinly slice the vegatables (Skin can be included) with a mandolin or peeler and try to get as wide and thin as you can.
  3. Place sliced vegatables into a bowl and coat with oil evenly
  4. Place vegatables on as many baking trays as necessary in a single layer. I use 3-4 trays.
  5. Place in oven and bake for 25-30 minutes until they are crisp and lightly browned.
  6. Remove from oven and when cooled toss with sea salt.
  7. Store in an airtight container to keep fresh.
