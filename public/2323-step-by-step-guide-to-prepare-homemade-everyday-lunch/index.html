The ingredients needed to make Everyday lunch:
  1. Take 1 chicken breast
  2. Take 1 medium onion
  3. Take 1/2 cup couscous
  4. Get 1/2 carrot
  5. Take 1/2 parsnip
  6. Take 1 clove garlic
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp soy sauce
  10. Prepare to taste salt and pepper
  11. Make ready 1 clove grated garlic
  12. Make ready 1/2 head broccoli

Instructions to make Everyday lunch:
  1. Marinate chicken during the night with olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, grated garlic, ginger, salt and pepper
  2. Cook onion until tender and add grated carrot and parsnip
  3. Boil water and add salt and broccoli.
  4. Add chicken breast to carrot, parsnip and onion and cook
  5. Boil water and add to half cup of couscous
  6. Serve and enjoy
