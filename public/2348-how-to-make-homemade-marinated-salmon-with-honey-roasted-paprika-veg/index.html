Instructions to make Marinated salmon with honey roasted paprika veg:
  1. Preheat the oven to gas mark 7
  2. Slice the veg (carrots, parsnips, celery, peppers, cauliflower leaves) into long thin strips
  3. Place the veg into a bowl and mix with the honey, oil, paprika, salt and pepper then place onto foil lined baking trays into the oven for 50 minutes
  4. Take the salmon and marinate in the lemon juice, oil, paprika salt and pepper for 20-30 minutes
  5. 20 minutes in, toss the veg and move the trays around to ensure even cooking of the veg
  6. 15-20 minutes before the end of the veg cooking, fry the salmon in a frying pan, 8 minutes on each side in a hot pan.
  7. Once cooked, plate up!
