The ingredients needed to make Chunky chicken soup:
  1. Prepare 1 X Butternut squash
  2. Get 2 X Leeks
  3. Prepare 1 X Swede
  4. Get 1 X Parsnips
  5. Make ready 3 X Carrots
  6. Prepare 5 X Celery Sticks
  7. Make ready 5 X potatoes
  8. Prepare 3 Chicken breasts
  9. Take 5 X vegetable stock cubes
  10. Get Salt
  11. Take Pepper

Steps to make Chunky chicken soup:
  1. Chop all the vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Add to large pan & bring to the boil & add the stock cubes. Season to taste with salt and pepper..
  3. Simmer until cooked.
  4. Remove half into a large bowl & blitz with a food processor untill smooth.
  5. Once blitzed add to the chunky mixture & mix together..
  6. Eat & enjoy - can be frozen.
