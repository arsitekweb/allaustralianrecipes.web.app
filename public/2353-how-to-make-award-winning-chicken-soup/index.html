The ingredients needed to make Chicken Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 large chicken (around 2.25kg), either left whole or quartered (I freeze raw chicken carcasses and trimmings from other dishes and add these to my soup for extra flavour)

  3. Prepare 2 medium onions, peeled
  4. Prepare 2 large leeks, halved lengthways, dark green part removed

  9. Take 1/2 small raw beetroot, peeled (optional)
  10. Prepare 1 handful parsley on the stem, no need to chop it
  11. Take 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, plus extra to taste
  12. Prepare Cold water
  13. Prepare Chopped parsley to garnish
