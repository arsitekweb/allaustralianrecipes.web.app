The ingredients needed to make Broad Beans Tawa Fry:

  2. Get 1 tbsp oil
  3. Take To taste salt
  4. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Take 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp coriander powder

Steps to make Broad Beans Tawa Fry:
  1. First of all wash the beans well.
  2. Then slightly trim both the ends of each bean so that the strings which might be present in both the sides can easily be removed.
  3. Transfer them to a pot with some water. Add in a pinch of salt and turmeric powder. Bring it to a boil over medium heat for 5-6 minutes.
  4. Drain and marinate them with ginger garlic paste, turmeric powder, chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander powder and salt.
  5. Heat a Tawa. Sprinkle oil all over and gently place the marinated beans. Shallow fry them over a low flame for 4-5 minutes or until brown spots appear on them.
  6. Remove and serve hot with lunch as a side dish.
