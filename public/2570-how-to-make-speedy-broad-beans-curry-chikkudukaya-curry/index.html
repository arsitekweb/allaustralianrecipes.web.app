The ingredients needed to make Broad beans curry/chikkudukaya curry:
  1. Prepare 250 gms broad beans
  2. Make ready 1 chopped tomato
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp sugar
  4. Get 2 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Get 1 tsp mustard,cumin mix
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp hing
  8. Prepare 2 tsp roasted peanut powder
  9. Make ready To taste Salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup Water

Steps to make Broad beans curry/chikkudukaya curry:
  1. In a kadhai take oil add mustard,cumin. Then add hing, turmeric and chopped tomato and saute till soft
  2. Add beans,red chilli powder,salt,sugar,water and cook covered for 10 mins. Stir occasionally.
  3. Once cooked add peanut powder mix well and cook for 2-5 mins and serve.
