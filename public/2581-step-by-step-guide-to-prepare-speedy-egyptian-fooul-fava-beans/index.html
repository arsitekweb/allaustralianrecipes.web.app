The ingredients needed to make Egyptian Fooul (Fava beans):
  1. Make ready 16 oz Fava Bean can
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  4. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  5. Take 1 tsp paprika powder
  6. Take 1 tsp ground cumin
  7. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. Get 1 tsp onion powder
  9. Take 1 tsp oregano

Instructions to make Egyptian Fooul (Fava beans):
  1. Boil Fava beans in half cup water
  2. Keep water if you want more soup like. Otherwise, drain as you like after it boils.
  3. After boil, keep on stove on low heat.
  4. Add in olive oil, and remainder of ingredients.
  5. Stir everything and mash Fava beans.
