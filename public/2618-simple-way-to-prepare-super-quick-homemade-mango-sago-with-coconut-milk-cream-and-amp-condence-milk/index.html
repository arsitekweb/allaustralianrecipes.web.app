Steps to make MANGO SAGO WITH COCONUT MILK CREAM & CONDENCE MILK:
  1. INSTRUCTIONS - * In a pot, bring 1 liter of water to boil. Add the small sago pearls and cook for 15 minutes or until translucent. (Check package instructions.) Stir occasionally to prevent from sticking together.
  2. Turn off heat and strain the sago pearls using a fine sieve. Rinse thoroughly until the sago pearls are no longer hot to touch. Transfer to a bowl and soak in little water.
  3. Reserve about a cup of the mango cubes for later as toppings. Place the remaining in a blender. turns into a smooth puree.
  4. Drain water from the cooked sago pearls and transfer in a larger bowl. Add the mango puree and coconut milk cream & condance milk and mix with a spoon or spatula until well blended.
  5. Divide the mango sago into 6-8 serving bowls and top each with the reserved mango cubes. Almond & pistachio Chill for at least an hour before serving.
