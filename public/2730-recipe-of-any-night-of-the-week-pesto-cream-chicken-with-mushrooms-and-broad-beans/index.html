Instructions to make Pesto cream chicken with mushrooms and broad beans:
  1. Dice the chicken breasts
  2. Grill the chicken breast until slightly golden on both sides (turn half way). Meanwhile carry on with rest of recipe.
  3. Pop the beans out and give them a wash
  4. Boil pasta (see packet, mine was 12 mins). Add broad beans when 3 minutes remaining
  5. Slice mushrooms and finely chop garlic. Fry with a little olive oil until soft.
  6. Add chicken and sauce ingredients to mushroom pan. Stir and heat through until everything is hot.
  7. Serve on pasta. Add some cheese on top!
