The ingredients needed to make Spicy chicken udon noodles:
  1. Prepare 350 g chicken breast in cubes (use tofu for a vegetarian option)
  2. Get 1 small courgette
  3. Prepare 100 g podded broad beans
  4. Get 1 spiralized carrot
  5. Prepare Sesame oil(rapeseed works too)
  6. Get 4 portions ready to go in the wok udon noodles
  7. Take For the sauce
  8. Take 4 tspoon rice (or sesame oil)
  9. Take 2 tspoon of chilli puree
  10. Take 3 teaspoons barbecue sauce (I used honey BBQ)
  11. Prepare 3 teaspoons red miso

Instructions to make Spicy chicken udon noodles:
  1. Heat sesame oil in wok and add chicken cubes. Add veg cut in small pieces, or spiralized. (You can add the veg you like most instead but remember to increase amount of sauce if you add more than suggested)
  2. Mix the different ingredients to make sauce while chicken cooks.
  3. Add noodles to the wok and loosen up
  4. Finally add prepared sauce to the wok, heat through and serve
