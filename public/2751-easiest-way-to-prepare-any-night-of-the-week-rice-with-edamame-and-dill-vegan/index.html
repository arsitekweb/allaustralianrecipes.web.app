The ingredients needed to make Rice with edamame and dill - vegan:
  1. Get 1 cup rice
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Make ready Sea salt
  4. Make ready 2 cups - maybe more - water
  5. Make ready 1.5 cups edamame/ broad beans/ peas, shelled - fresh or frozen
  6. Take 1 large handful of dill
  7. Take Juice of 1/2 lemon

The vegetables should be cooked by now. Add the rice to the dill mixture and mix it thoroughly. Instructions to make Rice with edamame and dill - vegan:
  1. In a pan with a lid, put the rice, oil, water and generous pinch of salt.
  2. Cover the pan, bring to the boil and simmer until most of the liquid is absorbed and the rice is almost cooked. The amount of simmering time the rice needs depends on the type of rice. It can be 20-30 mins.
  3. Add the edamame and dill. Stir then cover again and simmer for 5 mins.
