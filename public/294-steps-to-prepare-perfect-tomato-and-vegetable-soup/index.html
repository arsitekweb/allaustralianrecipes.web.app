The ingredients needed to make Tomato and vegetable soup:
  1. Get 1/4 cauliflower
  2. Prepare 1 carrot
  3. Get 1/2 parsnip
  4. Prepare 1/2 can chopped tomato
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp tomato paste
  6. Prepare 1/2 onion
  7. Take 1 clove garlic
  8. Take 500 ml veg stock
  9. Make ready Basil
  10. Make ready Oregano
  11. Get Salt
  12. Get Pepper
  13. Get Chilli (optional)
