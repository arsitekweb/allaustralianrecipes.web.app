The ingredients needed to make Spicy Fava Beans with burger bun

#stayingathome:

  1. Get 1 can Fava beans
  2. Prepare to taste Salt
  3. Get 1 tsp olive oil
  4. Get 1 tbsp tomato paste
  5. Get 1/4 of turmeric powder
  6. Take 1/4 of chilli powder
  7. Get 2 tsp Fresh lime juice
  8. Get Fresh coriander hand full
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp Fried onion (optional)
