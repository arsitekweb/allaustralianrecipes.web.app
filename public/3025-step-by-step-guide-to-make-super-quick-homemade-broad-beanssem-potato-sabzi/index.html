The ingredients needed to make Broad Beans(Sem) Potato Sabzi:
  1. Get 250 gm Broad beans/Sem
  2. Get 2 Potatoes
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp Onion Garlic paste
  4. Take 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  5. Get 1 tsp Red Chilli powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Fenugreek seeds
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp Mustard oil
  8. Make ready to taste Salt
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp chopped Coriander leaves

Steps to make Broad Beans(Sem) Potato Sabzi:
  1. Wash and cut beans and potatoes.
  2. Heat mustard oil on a kadahi.
  3. Add chopped beans and potatoes.
  4. Fry them till raw smell goes and beans become soft.
  5. Remove beans from oil and add more oil in kadahi.
  6. Crackle fenugreek seeds and then add onion garlic paste.
  7. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and salt.
  8. Mix all well and cook on high flame till oil starts separate.
  9. Add fried beans+potatoes and mix well.
  10. Cook for sometimes and add water.
  11. Cook on low flame till beans and potatoes done.
  12. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with rice.
