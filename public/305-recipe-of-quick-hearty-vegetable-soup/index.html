The ingredients needed to make Hearty Vegetable Soup:
  1. Take 1 tbsp butter
  2. Take 1 onion
  3. Get 2 large potatoes
  4. Get 2 large carrots
  5. Take 1 parsnip
  6. Get 1.5 litres vegetable stock (2 stock cubes)
  7. Prepare 4 stalks celery
