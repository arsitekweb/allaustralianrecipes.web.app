The ingredients needed to make Stir fried broad beans with Chinese sausage:
  1. Prepare 500 g broad beans without pods
  2. Prepare 2 là cháng Chinese sausages
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Shaoxing rice wine
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions to make Stir fried broad beans with Chinese sausage:
  1. Slice the sausages thinly and stir-fry in oil.
  2. Add the broad beans and stir-fry until they start to blister and the sausage starts to caramelize.
  3. Add the rice wine, stir for a few moments, letting the alcohol evaporate.
  4. Take off the heat and add the sesame oil.
