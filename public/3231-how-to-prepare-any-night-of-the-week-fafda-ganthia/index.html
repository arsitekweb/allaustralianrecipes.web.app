The ingredients needed to make Fafda ganthia:
  1. Take 150 gram Besan (gram flour)
  2. Take 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida
  3. Take 1/2 teaspoon saji na ful
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 2 tablespoon oil
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon ajma
  7. Get 5 tablespoon water
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps to make Fafda ganthia:
  1. Sieve the flour in a wide dish - In a bowl add asafoetida, salt, saji na ful and 3 tablespoon of water. Mix everything up. - Make a well in the flour and add the mix and oil and make dough using 1 tablespoon water at a time. Make a soft dough.
  2. Now add ajma and black pepper powder and knead for 5 minutes or until it stops sticking and is pliable. - On a wooden board take a small ball and press it oblong way and using your palm press it and slide to make fafda. This takes a bit of practice. 😄😄 Using a sharp oiled knife slide the fafda. Deep fry until lightly golden. Sprinkle asafoetida and enjoy with a cup of tea.
