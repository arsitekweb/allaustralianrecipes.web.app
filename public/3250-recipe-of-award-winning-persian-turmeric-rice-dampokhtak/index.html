The ingredients needed to make Persian turmeric rice (Dampokhtak):
  1. Take 2 cups rice, rinsed under cold water
  2. Get 1 cup dried yellow fava beans, cooked boiled
  3. Take 1 large onion, sliced thin
  4. Get 1 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Get to taste Salt and chili flakes
  6. Get Butter

Dampokhtak دمیختک is a traditional Persian turmeric rice. It's made with fried onion, dried yellow Fava beans, rice and turmeric. It's one of the easiest and simplest Iranian rice dishes. Dampokhtak, cooked rice with beans, turmeric is the main spice of this dish, so it is colored in yellow!
