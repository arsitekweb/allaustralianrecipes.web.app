The ingredients needed to make Broad beans curry (chikkudu Kaya kura):
  1. Make ready 2 cups sliced Broad beans
  2. Prepare 4-5 Green chilli
  3. Make ready 1 big onion
  4. Prepare 1/2 table spoon ginger garlic paste
  5. Get 4-5 curry leaves
  6. Take Handful coriander leaves
  7. Get Pinch turmeric
  8. Prepare 1 table spoon chilli powder
  9. Get 1/2 table spoon coriander powder
  10. Make ready 1/2 table spoon cumin powder
  11. Prepare 1/2 table spoon mustard and cumin seeds
  12. Get to taste Salt
  13. Get 1/2 glass milk

Steps to make Broad beans curry (chikkudu Kaya kura):
  1. Boiled broad beans
  2. Slice onions, curry leaves, coriander leaves
  3. Grind Green chilli
  4. Take a pan and keep it on the stove. Pour oil, add mustard and cumin seeds, once it starts crackle add onions, curry leaves, salt and turmeric. Roast it in low flame. Add ginger garlic paste, add chilli paste. Roast it.
  5. Once the onion getting brown add broad beans, keep in the low flame. Close it with a lid. Again open it, add chilli powder, mix it, add coriander powder, cumin powder. Mix it. Once the Curry cooked properly add milk.
  6. Once the milk evoporates add coriander leaves, mix it. Take it into a bowl and serve hot with rice /chapathi /roti.
