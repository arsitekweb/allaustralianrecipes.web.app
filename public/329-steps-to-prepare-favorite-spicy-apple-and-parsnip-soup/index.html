The ingredients needed to make Spicy Apple and parsnip soup:
  1. Get 1 onion
  2. Take 5 parsnips
  3. Prepare 2 potatoes
  4. Make ready Teaspoon ground cumin
  5. Take Teaspoon chili powder
  6. Get 2 cardamom pods
  7. Take 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon paprika
  9. Make ready 1 chopped apple
  10. Take 1 tablespoon olive oil
  11. Make ready 1 veggie stock cube
  12. Get Pint water
  13. Prepare 1 teaspoon brown mustard seeds. Optional
  14. Make ready Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Spicy Apple and parsnip soup:
  1. Add all the veg chopped. No need to peel root veg. Heat pan and start to dry fry.
  2. Add spices and oil.
  3. Chilli and salt and pepper.
  4. Continue to fry until veg is coated and golden.
  5. Add stock and water and mustard seeds.
  6. Simmer for 20 mins until veg soft.
  7. When cooked blitz with hand blender.
  8. Serve with warm pitta and s sprinkle of cayenne pepper. Enjoy.
