The ingredients needed to make Eva's spicy mushroom soup:
  1. Get 150 g spicy chorizo, sliced
  2. Take 250 g fresh mushrooms, sliced
  3. Get 2 large carrots, sliced
  4. Take 2 parsnips, sliced

  8. Prepare to taste salt, pepper
  9. Take 1 1/2 L chicken stock
  10. Prepare 2-3 tbs oil
  11. Make ready 1 tbs cornstarch
