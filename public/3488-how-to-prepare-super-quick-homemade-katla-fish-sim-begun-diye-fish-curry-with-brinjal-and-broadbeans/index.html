The ingredients needed to make Katla fish sim begun diye/fish curry with brinjal and broadbeans:
  1. Get 4 pieces KATLA fish
  2. Get 1 small brinjal cut in lenghwise
  3. Prepare 6-7 broadbeans cut half and then lengthwise
  4. Get 1 green chilli slitted
  5. Get 1 tbsp cumin powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp coriander powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tbsp chilli powder
  9. Get Bunch coriander leaves
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon black jeera
  11. Make ready to taste Salt
  12. Prepare mustard oil for fish fry and curry
  13. Prepare 6-7 pieces Dal fritters

Steps to make Katla fish sim begun diye/fish curry with brinjal and broadbeans:
  1. Wash the fish pieces and marinate it with turmeric and salt..keep aside for 10 mins.Now cut the veggies.
  2. Now turn on the gas oven. take a pan or kadai..Put oil in it and fry the fish. Now in the same oil fry dal fritters also..keep aside fried fish and dal fritters.
  3. Now in the same oil,add black jeera,brinjal,broadbeans, green chilli fry them with turmeric and salt..
  4. Now make a paste of all the masalas and put in on the veggies and now stir them properly and now add the fried fish in it and cover the lid cook for 7 mins..Now add coriander leaves. Your fish curry is ready to serve.
