The ingredients needed to make Broad beans potato curry:
  1. Prepare 250 gm Broad beans
  2. Make ready 3 tablespoon Oil
  3. Take 1/4 tsp Cumin seeds
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp Mustard seeds
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp Chana dal
  6. Get 2 Onion finely chopped
  7. Take 3 Tomato finely chopped
  8. Prepare 1 Green chilli chopped
  9. Get 1/8 tsp Turmeric powder
  10. Get 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder
  11. Get 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
  12. Get Salt as per your taste

Steps to make Broad beans potato curry:
  1. Wash and remove both ends of broad beans and divide it in pieces.
  2. Wash peel and cut potatoes in cubes.
  3. Heat a wok and add oil in it.
  4. Now add cumin seeds mustard seeds and chana dal. Saute for a few seconds.
  5. Now add Onion and mix it.
  6. When Onion becomes golden brown add tomato and green chilli. Cook it well.
  7. When tomato becomes soft and mushy add red chilli powder turmeric powder and coriander powder.mix it nicely.
  8. When spices starts leaving oil add broad beans and potato. Mix everything properly.
  9. Now add salt as per your taste. Cook it.
  10. When veggie turn soft turn off the flame.
  11. Your broad beans and potato curry is ready to serve.
