The ingredients needed to make Bajre ke Aatay ki kabab:
  1. Prepare 1/2 kg beef mince
  2. Take 1/2 kg bajre ka Aata
  3. Prepare Salt as per taste
  4. Make ready 5 Tbsp Small Green chilies paste
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Ginger paste
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Garlic paste
  7. Take 1 Tbsp Vinegar
  8. Get Cumin 1st
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  10. Take Coriander 1 hand ful
  11. Get 3 Tbsp Hara lahsan finely chopped
  12. Prepare 1 big onion finely chopped
  13. Prepare Oil for deep frying
