The ingredients needed to make Chiken tikka karahi:
  1. Take 1 chiken
  2. Get 1 medium bowl yougart
  3. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp red chili powder
  5. Get 1 tsp coriender powder
  6. Take 1 tsp haldi

  8. Take 1 tbsp ginger paste

  10. Make ready 1 tsp black paper

  12. Get 1 cup oil

Steps to make Chiken tikka karahi:
  1. Take All the ingredients according to recipie
  2. Add venigar soya souce n ginger garlic paste in chiken bowl n mix well then add all dry ingredients in yougart bowl n mix well
  3. Then add yougart mixture in chiken n mix well n let it rest for 30 to 50 mins
  4. Take karahi n add oil in it then add marinated chiken in hot oil
