Instructions to make Eggless Donuts:
  1. Take a bowl add flour, powdered sugar, baking powder, baking soda, butter, little bit water and milk for make a smooth dough.
  2. Now take rest for this 30 minutes
  3. After that roll the dough in thick manner and cut it as donuts shape.
  4. Now heat the pan with medium flame and fry the donuts until it gets brown in colour.
  5. On the other hand microwave the dark and white chocolate with fresh cream for 30 Seconds.
  6. Now dip and pour the donuts with this hot chocolate syrup and add the sprinkles as per your choice
  7. Let them cool and served with love
