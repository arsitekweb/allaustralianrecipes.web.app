The ingredients needed to make Hot and spicy potato soup with homemade pasta:
  1. Get 150 g smoked spicy sausage (Chorizo) sliced
  2. Take 4 rashes of smoked bacon, cut into small pieces
  3. Take 1 onion finely chopped
  4. Take 1 bell pepper, sliced
  5. Make ready 4 medium size potato, cubed
  6. Get 2-3 carrots, sliced
  7. Get 2 parsnips, sliced
  8. Take 1 level tbsp of paprika powder
  9. Take 1 large tomato
  10. Make ready pepper
  11. Get salt
  12. Get 1 bay leaf
  13. Take oil to fry onion
  14. Make ready For the pasta:
  15. Get 1 egg
  16. Prepare 150 g flour
  17. Prepare salt

Steps to make Hot and spicy potato soup with homemade pasta:
  1. In a large pot fry onion, bacon and sausage for a few minutes. Take off heat and add paprika powder and chopped tomatoes, stir and add 2 L (approx 4 pint) of water and bring to the boil, add rest of the vegetables, salt, pepper and bay leaf. Simmer gently until all vegetables are almost soft.
  2. In the meantime, prepare pasta by combining flour, egg and salt. Make a dough and roll it out very thinly, cut into approximately 5 cm squares and fry them quickly in a frying pan. Remove pasta and add to the soup, let is boil again until everything is cooked. Season to taste and serve with freshly baked bread roll, or garlic bread. Enjoy.
