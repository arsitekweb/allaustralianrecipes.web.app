The ingredients needed to make Hearty Vegetable Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
  2. Prepare 1 onion
  3. Get 2 large potatoes
  4. Take 2 large carrots
  5. Prepare 1 parsnip
  6. Take 1.5 litres vegetable stock (2 stock cubes)
  7. Get 4 stalks celery

Steps to make Hearty Vegetable Soup:

  2. Fry onion and potato in the butter in a large pot for 2 minutes
  3. Cover and sweat for 8 minutes
  4. Add stock and the rest of the vegetables, and simmer for 29 minutes
