The ingredients needed to make Palak mishmash curry (Palong shaaker ghonto):
  1. Take 1 bunch Palak leaves
  2. Make ready 7-8 flat broad beans
  3. Take 1 raddish
  4. Take 1/2 brinjal
  5. Take 1 potato
  6. Prepare 5-6 urad dal dumplings
  7. Take 1/4 tsp paanch phoron
  8. Take To taste salt and sugar
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp mustard oil
  10. Take 1 dry red chilli
  11. Take 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
