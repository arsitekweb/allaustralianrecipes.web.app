Steps to make cherry & Walnut Brownies:
  1. preheat oven on temp 200
  2. Put all ingredients of Brownies(except cherries & walnuts) in a big bowl, mix well till smooth
  3. fold in cherries & walnuts
  4. put the mixture in a square mould
  5. put in oven for 30 minutes
  6. meanwhile, melt all ingredients for topping
  7. after 30 minutes(or less) take the brownies out of oven, cut into pieces of your choice and drizzle the toping over it
  8. decorate with dried cherries & walnuts
