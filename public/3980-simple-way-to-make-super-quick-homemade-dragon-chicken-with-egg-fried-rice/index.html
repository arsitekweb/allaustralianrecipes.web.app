The ingredients needed to make Dragon Chicken with egg fried rice:
  1. Make ready For marination:
  2. Take 500 gm chicken boneless
  3. Get 1 egg beaten
  4. Get 2 tbsp corn flour
  5. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Get 2 tsp soya sauce
  8. Take 2 tsp chilli sauce
  9. Make ready For the gravy:
  10. Take 2 tsp dark soya sauce
  11. Get 1/2 cup ketchup
  12. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli paste
  13. Get 2 tbsp oil
  14. Take 2 garlic cloves crushed
  15. Make ready 1 capsicum cut into cubes
  16. Prepare 1 onion cut into cubes
  17. Get 5-6 red chillies
  18. Get to taste Salt
  19. Take 1 hand ful of chopped green onions

Add ingredients listed under marination - cornflour, all purpose flour, ginger and garlic paste, chilli powder, pepper powder, soy. Dragon chicken recipe with step by step pictures. Now add in the fried chicken and toss well in the sauce. Add in chopped coriander or springonion and mix well.

Instructions to make Dragon Chicken with egg fried rice:
  1. Marinate the chicken and let rest for atleast 10 mins.
  2. Then deep fry the chicken on medium heat. Do not over fry.
  3. Keep the chicken aside. In a non stick wok, add oil and crushed garlic and saute.
  4. Add the onions and capsicum and saute for a minute. Then add the chicken and saute.
  5. Now add all the sauces gradually, mixing everything together.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve with egg fried rice.
