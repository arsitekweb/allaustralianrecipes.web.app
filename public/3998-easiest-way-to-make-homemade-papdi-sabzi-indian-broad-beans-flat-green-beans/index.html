The ingredients needed to make Papdi Sabzi (Indian Broad Beans) Flat Green Beans:
  1. Make ready 250 gms Flat Green Beans
  2. Get 2 tbsp Mustard Oil
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  4. Make ready pinch Asafoetida
  5. Take 10-15 cloves Garlic (Sliced)
  6. Prepare 2 " piece Ginger (Sliced)
  7. Get 3 Long Green Chillies (Halved)
  8. Make ready 1 sprig Curry
  9. Take 1 Onion (Chopped)
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  11. Take 2 tsp Everest Sabzi Masala
  12. Get as required Salt

Steps to make Papdi Sabzi (Indian Broad Beans) Flat Green Beans:
  1. Clean and wash the flat green beans. Cut into small pieces. Steam it in a rice cooker or steamer and set aside.
  2. In a cooking pan, heat mustard oil. Add cumin seeds and let it sizzle. Add asafoetida. Add chopped garlic and saute till light brown.
  3. Add ginger and saute.
  4. Add green chillies and curry leaves and saute.
  5. Add onions and saute till the onions are translucent.
  6. Add turmeric powder, sabzi masala and salt and saute for a minute.
  7. Add the steamed beans and cover and cook for 6 to 8 minutes.
  8. Ready to serve.
