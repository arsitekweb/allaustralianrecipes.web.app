The ingredients needed to make Yellow peas radish and flat beans dal:
  1. Make ready 150 gm yellow peas daal soaked and boiled
  2. Make ready 1 radish sliced
  3. Get 5-6 flat beans sliced
  4. Make ready 1 tsp nigella seeds
  5. Prepare 1 red chilli whole
  6. Take To taste salt and sugar
  7. Make ready As needed oil

Steps to make Yellow peas radish and flat beans dal:
  1. Heat oil and temper nigella seeds and red chilli
  2. Add sliced radish and beans and saute well
  3. Add salt and turmeric powder and cover till it is soft
  4. Now pour the daal and mix well adding sugar
  5. Serve it with rice
