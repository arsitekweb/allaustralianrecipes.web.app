The ingredients needed to make Avarakkai curry (flat beans):
  1. Get 1.5 cups chopped avarakkai (flat beans)
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  3. Get 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  4. Take 1/2 tsp urad dal
  5. Get 1/4 tsp turmeric
  6. Take 2 dry red chilli broken
  7. Make ready 2 pinches asafoetida or hing
  8. Make ready 7-8 small to medium sized curry leaves
  9. Take 1/2 cup water or add as required
  10. Make ready 3 tbsp grated fresh coconut or desiccated coconut flakes
  11. Get to taste salt

Instructions to make Avarakkai curry (flat beans):
  1. Heat a pan. Add oil. Add mustard seeds. once it sputters add urad dal and red chilli, hing and curry leaves.
  2. Add veggie with a pinch of turmeric, salt to taste.
  3. Close the pan with a lid and allow to cook till it becomes soft.
  4. Once cooked, add coconut and check for salt.
