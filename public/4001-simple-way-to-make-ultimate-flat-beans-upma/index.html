The ingredients needed to make Flat beans Upma:
  1. Get 1 cup flat beans
  2. Take 1 cup Upma rava
  3. Take 1 chopped onion
  4. Make ready 3 green chillies
  5. Take 1 piece ginger
  6. Make ready 1/2 spoon mustard seeds
  7. Prepare 1/2 spoon urad dal
  8. Take 1/2 spoon chana dal
  9. Make ready 3 tablespoon cooking oil3 cups water
  10. Take to taste Salt
  11. Get 1 spoon Lemon juice
