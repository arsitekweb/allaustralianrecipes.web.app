The ingredients needed to make Gota sedhdho:
  1. Get 1 cup whole green moong dal
  2. Prepare 1 cup whole black urad dal
  3. Get as required small sized whole veggies (potato, baby spinach, brinjal. Flat beans, peas, sweet potato)
  4. Take 2 tablespoons grated ginger
  5. Get 2 tablespoon fennel seed
  6. Get 5-6 whole slitted red chilli
  7. Get 1 tablespoon sugar
  8. Make ready to taste salt
  9. Take 2-4 tablespoons mustard oil

Steps to make Gota sedhdho:
  1. Wash and soak the dal overnight. Wash all the veggies thoroughly.
  2. In a heavy bottom vessel put dal and veggies along with all the masalas. Add sufficient water so that all the stuff deep properly. Slow cook them on low flame till dal and veggies are well cooked.
  3. Check them time to time. When done drizzle the mustard oil on top of it and mix well.
  4. Healthy and tasty gota sedhdho is ready. Have it as it is or with steamed rice.
