The ingredients needed to make Foxtail Millet Upma:
  1. Take 1/2 cup foxtail Millet / Kangni
  2. Get 1 cup chopped assorted veggies (beans, carrot, peas, flat beans, etc), chopped
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp. oil
  4. Prepare 1 tsp. mustard seeds
  5. Make ready 1 sprig curry leaves
  6. Make ready 2 green chillies, slit
  7. Make ready 1 tsp. urad dal (split black gram lentil)
  8. Take 1 tsp. ginger, grated
  9. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  10. Prepare to taste salt
  11. Prepare 1 tsp. sambar powder
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp. turmeric powder
  13. Make ready 1 tsp. ghee
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp. coriander leaves, chopped

Instructions to make Foxtail Millet Upma:
  1. Soak the millet in sufficient water for 1-2 hours. Wash well and drain. Keep aside. Heat oil in a pan and temper with mustard seeds.
  2. After it stops spluttering, add the curry leaves, urad dal, green chilies, ginger and onion. Saute till the onion turns translucent.
  3. Add all the chopped veggies, salt and turmeric powder. Mix well and cook, covered till the veggies are soft.
  4. Add the drained millet,
  5. 2 cups water and sambar powder. Cover & simmer till the millet is cooked and the moisture is all absorbed. Switch off the flame and add ghee. Stir and garnish with coriander leaves.
  6. Serve with coconut chutney or pickle.

The best combination for this upma is coconut chutney or brinjal.
