The ingredients needed to make Baked Appe Muffins:
  1. Get Ingredints for Batter:
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup Urad Dal
  3. Get 2 tblspn Chana dal , soaked in water for-1 hr
  4. Take 11/4th cup Flat rice/, soaked in water for 15 mins
  5. Make ready Methi soaked in water along with urad dal
  6. Prepare 1 cup Rice flour, soaked in 1 cup water for 15 mins
  7. Make ready Vegetables:
  8. Get 1/4th cup Chopped onion
  9. Make ready 2 tblspn Chopped/grated carrots
  10. Make ready 2 tblspn Chopped green beans
  11. Take 2 tblspn Chopped bell peppers
  12. Make ready 1/4th cup Chopped coriander
  13. Take to taste Salt
  14. Take Tampering:
  15. Prepare Bunch curry leaves
  16. Take 2 tbspn Chopped green chilli
  17. Take 1 tspn Mustard seeds
  18. Prepare 1 tspn Cumin seeds
  19. Prepare 2 cut 1:4 Whole dry red chilli
  20. Make ready 2 tblspn Oil for Tadka/Tampering
  21. Prepare 2 tblspn Oil for greasing the muffin/appe pan

Instructions to make Baked Appe Muffins:
  1. Make the Batter: - Grind all the soaked ingredients one by one. Now mix everything with rice flour and again grind all together to make a fine batter. - Ferment this batter in a bowl with tight lid at least for 8 hrs or till it gets fermented nicely.
  2. Add all other vegetables in the batter. Add salt and chopped coriander. Mix the batter well.
  3. Tadka/Tampering: In a pan heat oil for Tadka. Add whole red chilli cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let it splatter. Add curry leaves. Fry for a second. Add onions. Stir till just translucent. Cool a little then add to the fermented batter.
  4. Baking: - Pour ½ tspn of oil in all moulds. Then pour the batter in the mould till neck. Half fill one mould with water. Cover the muffin tray with same sized tray like a lid or with silver foil covered from all side. - Pre heat oven in 200degrees for 10 min. - Bake the appe covered for 15 mins. Take out the tray.
  5. Flip the appes and bake again for 5-7 mins or till all are cooked. Take off the lid/cover. - If you wish to brown the appes then bake it open for 3-4 min in hot oven.
  6. Appe are ready to serve. Best paired with coconut chutney.
