The ingredients needed to make Persian dill and fava beans rice with lamb shank (baghali polow BA mahichey):
  1. Prepare 4 cups rice
  2. Prepare 2 pieces lamb shanks
  3. Get 2 large onions, finely chopped
  4. Take 2 cups cooked fava beans
  5. Take 150 g dried dill (fresh dill)
  6. Get 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  7. Take 1 tbsp rose petals powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp cardamom
  9. Take 1/2 tsp turmeric
  10. Make ready Saffron threads
  11. Get 1 orange
  12. Make ready 1 lime
  13. Make ready 2 potatoes, thinly sliced
  14. Take 2 small stalks of parsley (it could be rosemary, thyme or parsley as well)
  15. Take Salt- chilli powder
  16. Get Oil
  17. Make ready Butter

Steps to make Persian dill and fava beans rice with lamb shank (baghali polow BA mahichey):
  1. Heat the oil in a pan. Add chopped onions with turmeric and cinnamon powder and sauté until soft.
  2. Wash and drain lamb shanks. Add the meat to the onions
  3. And fry for about 15 minutes or until turns brown.
  4. Zest the lime.
  5. In a bowl with orange juice, add lime peel add pinch of chili powder and saffron thread, mix all together.
  6. Add this juice to the fried meat. Pour 1 liter water into the meat pot and let it to cook until tender. Once it’s cooked season it with salt.
  7. Cook fava beans then peel the skin off of the beans. Set aside.
  8. Wash the rice. Pour the rice into salted water and soak it for about 1 hour. In a large pan, bring water to boil. Pour off salted water from rice and pour it into boiling water. Let to boil for 10 minutes. Add cooked beans just before strain the rice.
