The ingredients needed to make Sig's Spicy Carrot and Butter Bean Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 large onion,chopped
  2. Prepare 2 clove smoked garlic
  3. Get 450 grams carrots,peeled an thinly sliced
  4. Make ready 1 tin butter beans
  5. Take 1 tsp each ground coriander,ground cumin,paprika and hot chilli powder
  6. Make ready 900 ml vegetable stock
  7. Get 1 pinch salt and fresh ground pepper
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  9. Get 30 pecan nuts for garnish
  10. Get 1 handful broad beans for garnish,(optional)
  11. Prepare 20 ml olive oil

Instructions to make Sig's Spicy Carrot and Butter Bean Soup:
  1. Heat oil in pan,add onions,crushed garlic and carrots,cook for 5 min,add the ground spices simmer for 1 min,stir.Add stock and cook for about 40 minutes until carrots are cooked
  2. Add the butter beans, cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  3. Purée the soup with blender,season and reheat gently. Garnish with pecan nuts and/or broad beans.
