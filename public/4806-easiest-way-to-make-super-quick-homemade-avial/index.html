The ingredients needed to make Avial:
  1. Prepare 1 Carrot
  2. Make ready 5 Broad beans
  3. Make ready 1 Drumstick
  4. Take 250 gms Ash gourd
  5. Get 2 Green chilies
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup Curd
  8. Take 1/2 cup Coconut
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  10. Take 1 tbsp Coconut oil
  11. Get A handful Curry leaves
  12. Take 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder

Steps to make Avial:
  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Blend coconut, curd, green chillies and cumin seeds to a coarse paste
  3. Coarse paste,keep aside
  4. Chop the vegetables length wise and add a cup of water, add turmeric powder and salt. Cook on low heat.
  5. Once it's cooked add the ground paste, let it become thick on medium heat. Add the curry leaves and coconut oil, turn off heat
  6. Serve hot with rice or roti
