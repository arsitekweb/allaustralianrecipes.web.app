The ingredients needed to make Broad Bean Dip:
  1. Take 1 heaped cupful Broad Beans flesh *tough skin removed
  2. Take 1/2 clove Garlic *finely chopped
  3. Prepare 1/2-1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  4. Get Water
  5. Make ready 1/2-1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  6. Take Salt & Pepper
  7. Get 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan *optional

Steps to make Broad Bean Dip:
  1. Place Broad Beans flesh and Garlic in a saucepan, add Water just enough to cover. Cook 5 to 10 minutes or until Broad Beans are soft.
  2. Drain but save the liquid.
  3. Add Lemon Juice and Olive Oil. Using a stick blender, and adding some saved liquid as required, blend until smooth and the required texture is achieved.
  4. Season with Salt & Pepper. Add grated Parmesan but it is optional. Drizzle with extra Olive Oil and enjoy with nice crusty bread or grilled flat bread.
