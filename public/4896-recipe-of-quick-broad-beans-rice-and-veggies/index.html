The ingredients needed to make Broad beans rice and veggies:
  1. Take 100 g long white rice
  2. Get 1 medium onion
  3. Get 5 large cloves garlic
  4. Take 1 large potatoe
  5. Prepare 2 medium size courgette
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons olive oil
  7. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  8. Take Some dried thyme 🙂
  9. Prepare 250 green frozen fava beans (broad beans)
  10. Take 2 tablespoons neutral oil for sauteeing (i use sunflower oil)
  11. Take Water
  12. Prepare 1/2 tea spoon ground cumin
  13. Get 1/2 tea spoon ground cardimum
  14. Make ready Lemon Zest for garnish

Instructions to make Broad beans rice and veggies:
  1. Clean all vegetables thoroughly!
  2. Cut potatoes vertically half an inch size.
  3. Cut zuccinis vertically less than 1/4 inch
  4. In a non stick pan, put the sunflower oil and then place the potatoes after being seasoned with salt and pepper. Cover the pan
  5. After around 3 min, check that the potatoes are golden. Then flip them to the other side.
  6. Once both sides are golden, add tow crushed garlic gloves and a dash of thyme into the pan, and then pour in some water, simmer until they are soft. Remove from the pan.
  7. Fry zuccini peices until golden on both sides. Once done, set them aside.
  8. Cut the onion finely. Cut the the garlic gloves as well.
  9. In a pot, add in the olive oil, chopped onions and garlic, sautee them. And then add the beans. Cover the pot on a medium heat gas. Season with salt pepper, cumin, and cardimum.
  10. Add in the washed saiked rice, then water. Simmer until rice is done.
  11. Serve as shown in the picture, garnish with lemon zest and some balsamic vinager. 🙂
