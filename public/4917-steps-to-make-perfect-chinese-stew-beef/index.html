The ingredients needed to make Chinese Stew Beef:
  1. Take 400 g leg steak
  2. Make ready 4-5 anise
  3. Prepare 60 ml light soy sauce
  4. Take 80 ml rice wine or cooking wine
  5. Take 1 tbsp salt
  6. Take 2 tbsp sugar
  7. Make ready 3 sliced ginger
  8. Get 4 spring onion, only white part
  9. Take 1 tbsp Chinese prickly ash
  10. Take 1 cinnamon
  11. Take 2 tbsp broad bean paste
  12. Take 4 boiled egg
  13. Take Chinese noodle

Steps to make Chinese Stew Beef:
  1. Cut the leg steak into one bite size
  2. Stir fry the anise, ginger, spring onion, Chinese prickly ash, cinnamon and broad bean paste until all the smell come out
  3. Add the leg steak and keep stir fry until the beef is slight brown outside
  4. Add soy sauce and cooking wine, salt, sugar, then add hot water until cover all the beef, stew for 1h
  5. Add boiled egg, keep stewing for 30 minute
  6. Serve it with cooked noodles and spring onion on the top and enjoy!
