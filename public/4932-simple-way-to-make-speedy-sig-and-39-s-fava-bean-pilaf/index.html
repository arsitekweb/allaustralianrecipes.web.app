The ingredients needed to make sig's Fava Bean Pilaf:
  1. Take 50 ml vegetable oil
  2. Get 1 onion finely chopped
  3. Get 2 gloves garlic, finely.chopped or.crushed
  4. Get 2 tsp each,cumin and coriander seeds ,dry toaated for a few seconds and crushed with,mortar and pestle
  5. Prepare 10 cardamon pods

  8. Prepare 1 tsp turmeric

  10. Make ready 200 grams frozen or fresh fava ( broad) beans , remove membrane .
  11. Take 400 ml vegetable stock
  12. Get 50 grams butter
  13. Prepare 4 zucchini, ,cut into 1 inch slices,
  14. Get 1 pinch each salt and pepper
  15. Take 6 tsp creme fraiche
