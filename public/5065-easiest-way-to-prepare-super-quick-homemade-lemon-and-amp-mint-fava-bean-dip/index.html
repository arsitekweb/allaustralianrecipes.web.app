The ingredients needed to make Lemon & Mint Fava Bean Dip:
  1. Prepare 2 cups fava beans cooked
  2. Get 2 cloves garlic , pressed
  3. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon sea salt
  4. Take one lemon Juice of
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon white wine vinegar
  6. Take 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  7. Prepare 5 mint leaves
