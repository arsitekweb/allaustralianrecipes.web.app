The ingredients needed to make Surti Khamani:
  1. Take 200 gm besan/gram flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp Nimbu ful(citric acid)
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Soda Bicarbonate
  4. Make ready 3 tsp Small mustard seeds
  5. Make ready 3 tsp Sesame seeds
  6. Get 2-3 Green Chillies
  7. Make ready 3-4 tsp Powdered Sugar
  8. Take 1/2 tsp haldi/Turmeric powder
  9. Prepare To taste Salt
  10. Take 1 pinch hing/ asafoetida
  11. Take As needed Water for mixing the mixture
  12. Make ready 7-8 Curry leaves
  13. Prepare For garnishing
  14. Get handful nylon sev/bhujiya
  15. Make ready handful Pomegranate seeds
  16. Get 2 tsp coriander leaves finely chopped

Instructions to make Surti Khamani:
  1. In a bowl take besan add haldi and salt and mix it properly. Now add a cup of water mix it properly. Add Limbu ka ful and add little water mix properly.
