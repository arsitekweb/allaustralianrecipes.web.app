The ingredients needed to make Pork Vegetable Stew:
  1. Prepare 4 Pork Chops
  2. Prepare 2 large Onions
  3. Get 200 grams Carrots
  4. Make ready 200 grams Parsnips
  5. Get 1/2 head Broccoli
  6. Get 500 grams Potatoes
  7. Make ready 1 Green Pepper
  8. Get 4 teaspoons Dried Coriander
  9. Prepare 1 pint vegetable stock
  10. Prepare 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil
  11. Take 2 teaspoons ground Black Pepper

Instructions to make Pork Vegetable Stew:
  1. Wash, peel and chop the potatoes, Parsnips and Broccoli. Drop into a bowl of cold water.(stops the potatoes going brown and removes excess starch)
  2. Peel and chop the Onions, drop into a saucepan with the oil to start browning. Meanwhile cut the Pork Chops up into small chunks. Add to the Onions to brown off.
  3. Drain the Potatoes, Parsnips, Broccoli, add to a oven- proof dish, add Stock, Onions and Pork mix.Add dried Coriander and Black Pepper. Stir well and place in a medium hot Oven for about 40-50 minutes.
  4. Serve in bowls and enjoy.
