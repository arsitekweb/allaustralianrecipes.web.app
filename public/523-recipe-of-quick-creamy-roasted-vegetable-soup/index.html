  6. Take 2 carrots

  8. Get Salt, pepper
  9. Prepare Cayenne pepper
  10. Get Splash cream
  11. Get 1 clove minced garlic

Steps to make Creamy Roasted vegetable soup 🥔 🥕:
  1. Peel and chop veg, add to roasting dish season and drizzle with olive oil roast 190 for 45 mins
  2. Soften onion celery and garlic in a large saucepan. Add roasted veg, chicken stock and bring to boil simmer for 30 minutes
  3. Add cream just before serving and blend. Finish with a pinch of cayenne pepper. Can be left chunky is desired.
