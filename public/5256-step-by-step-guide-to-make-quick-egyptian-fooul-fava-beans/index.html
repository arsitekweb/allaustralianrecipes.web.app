The ingredients needed to make Egyptian Fooul (Fava beans):
  1. Take 16 oz Fava Bean can
  2. Get 3 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  4. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper
  5. Make ready 1 tsp paprika powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp ground cumin
  7. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp onion powder
