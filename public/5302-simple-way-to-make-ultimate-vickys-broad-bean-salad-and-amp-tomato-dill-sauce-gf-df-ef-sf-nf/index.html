The ingredients needed to make Vickys Broad Bean Salad & Tomato Dill Sauce, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Prepare 450 grams broad beans
  2. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Make ready 2 clove garlic, thinly sliced
  4. Get 2 tbsp concentrated tomato puree
  5. Get 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  6. Take 1 splash white wine
  7. Make ready 200 grams red & yellow cherry tomatoes
  8. Get 1 tbsp freshly chopped dill
  9. Take 1 tbsp freshly chopped rosemary
  10. Make ready 1 salt & pepper to taste
