The ingredients needed to make Mourola maach Sim diye:
  1. Get 200 grams Mourola fish
  2. Make ready 1 onion slices
  3. Get 1/2 tomato slices
  4. Make ready 2 green chilli slitted
  5. Get 6-8 broad beans cut in halves and then lengthwise
  6. Get 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp cumin powder
  8. Get to taste Salt
  9. Get 1/2 cumin seeds
  10. Take 2 garlic cloves chopped
  11. Make ready Bunch coriander leaves

Instructions to make Mourola maach Sim diye:
  1. Wash the fish and marinate it with turmeric and salt. Keep aside for 10 minutes.
  2. In the mean time cut the veggies. Now turn on the oven, and take a pan add mustard oil and now fry the fish and keep aside.
  3. Now add little more oil add cumin seeds in it,now add the veggies salt and turmeric. Now stir them properly. Cover the lid.
  4. Now add all the masalas.add little water. Cook for 5mins now add the fried fish in it. Mix them well and cook for more 5 to 7 mins..Add coriander leaves also.
