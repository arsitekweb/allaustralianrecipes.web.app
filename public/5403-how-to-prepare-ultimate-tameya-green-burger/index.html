The ingredients needed to make Tameya (green burger):
  1. Take 3 cups fava beans
  2. Make ready 6-8 scallions, minced
  3. Get 3-5 gloves of garlic crushed
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup fresh cilantro or parsley
  5. Take 1-2 tsp ground cumin
  6. Get 1/2 tsp ground coriander or 3 tbsp seeds
  7. Get 1-3 medium onion finely chopped
  8. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  9. Get Vegetable oil for deep frying

Steps to make Tameya (green burger):
  1. Soak the the fava beans in water overnight
  2. To prepare taameya: Drain fava beans. Combine cilantro, parsley, onion and garlic in a food processor. Process, scraping down the sides as necessary, until very finely chopped, about 1 minute. Add the fava beans and continue processing until a paste forms, about 3 minutes. Add 1 teaspoon coriander and salt; process for 1 minute more.
  3. If you use coriander powder: Pound the remaining 3 tablespoons coriander seeds in a mortar and pestle until cracked.
  4. Stir in sesame seeds and transfer to a plate.
  5. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat to 350 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with plastic wrap.
  6. Meanwhile, using 2 tablespoons each, form the fava bean mixture into 24 disks 1 1/2 inches wide. Lightly press both sides of the disks in the seed mixture and place on the prepared
  7. In batches, carefully drop the disks into the hot oil and cook, flipping once, until golden brown, about 4 minutes. Transfer the taameya to a baking sheet lined with paper towels.
