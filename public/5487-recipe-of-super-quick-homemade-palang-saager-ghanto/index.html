Instructions to make Palang saager ghanto:
  1. Peel wash and cut all the vegetables into cubes.
  2. Chop the spinach roughly.
  3. Heat mustard oil and fry the badi. keep aside.
  4. In the remaining oil tempered the oil with panchforon and slit green chilies.
  5. Add all the vegetables and saute for 5 minutes.
  6. Add salt and a little turmeric powder..
  7. Add spinach and badi.add ginger paste.
  8. Saute it until the spinach mix properly with all the vegetables.
  9. Cover the pan till all the vegetables soften and realise the water.Add half teaspoon sugar.
  10. Add 2 table spoon milk or cream before serving.
  11. Serve it with hot boiled rice.
