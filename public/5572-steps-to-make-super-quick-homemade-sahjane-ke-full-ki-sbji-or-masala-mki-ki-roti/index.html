The ingredients needed to make Sahjane ke full ki sbji or masala mki ki Roti:
  1. Get 1 kg sahjana ke ful
  2. Take 3 big tomatoes
  3. Get 4-5 hari lhsun
  4. Take to taste Salt
  5. Prepare 1 spoon Red chilli powder
  6. Take 1/2 spoon Haldi powder
  7. Take 1/4 bowl peas
  8. Prepare 4 Green chilli chopped
  9. Take 1 cup Curd (khata)
  10. Prepare 2 cup Maki ka flour
  11. Prepare 1 onion (chopped)
  12. Make ready Green coriander chopped
  13. Prepare 1 Tsp jeera
Instructions to make Sahjane ke full ki sbji or masala mki ki Roti:
  1. Sbji :- sahjane ke fulo ko saf kar ke 3times water mein boil krenge..
  2. 4th times cooker mein thoda water dal kar 5-6 whistle lgayenge..
  3. Ab ek kadae mein thoda oil add krenge usme jeera or green lhsun chopped kar ke dalenge.
  4. Ab ye ful ka extra water nikal kar dalenge ache se bhunenge..
  5. Green chilli,mtr or sare masale dal kar slow flame 🔥 par 10 min. Dhak kar rakh denge
  6. Ab tomato or curd b dal denge..
  7. Tomato glne tak pkayenge ready hei sbji…
  8. Upar se green corindar se garnish krenge…
  9. Roti - eske liye maki ke flour mein onion,green chilli,coriander, 1 tomato,hari lhsun ye sb chopped kar ke dalenge salt or mirchi to taste or 1 spoon. Oil dal kar water ki help se dough bana lenge…or
  10. Or rotiya tave par sek lenge.. Thanku…
