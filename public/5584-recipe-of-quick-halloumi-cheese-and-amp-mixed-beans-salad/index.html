The ingredients needed to make Halloumi cheese & mixed beans salad:
  1. Take 100 Grams Halloumi Cheese
  2. Get 3 Tablespoons broad beans
  3. Prepare 3 Tablespoons white beans
  4. Make ready 3 Tablespoons kidney beans
  5. Make ready 3 Tablespoons chickpeas
  6. Get 2 Tablespoons Green sprouts
  7. Take 1 onion sliced
  8. Make ready coriander for garnishing
  9. Make ready 1 Teaspoon Sesame seeds (dry roasted)
  10. Get 1 Tablespoon Olive oil
  11. Get 1 Tablespoon honey
  12. Take 1 Teaspoon Mustard (Crushed)
  13. Make ready 1 Teaspoon lemon juice
  14. Make ready 1 Teaspoon black pepper freshly cracked
  15. Get To Taste salt

Halloumi or haloumi (/ h ə ˈ l uː m i /) is a semi-hard, unripened, brined cheese made from a mixture of goat's and sheep's milk, and sometimes also cow's milk. It has a high melting point and so can easily be fried or grilled. This property makes it a popular meat substitute. Steps to make Halloumi cheese & mixed beans salad:
  1. Soak all the beans for 8 hrs and pressure cook them for 5-7 mins or 2 whistles only.
  2. Cut the halloumi cheese into 2 inch long strips, add little olive oil on a pan and grill the halloumi cheese from both sides. keep aside
  3. In a deep bowl mix all the beans & slice onions.
  4. Whisk the mustard powder with little water for 2 mins, it should become light and color should change to white. This will be used in the dressing
  5. Prepare dressing by mixing all the ingredients (olive oil, Honey, Lemon juice, mustard, freshly cracked pepper &Salt). Mix the dressing with the help of a whisk for a minute.
  6. Add this dressing in the beans & onions.Toss the salad Garnish with sesame seeds which are dry roasted Serve chilled.
