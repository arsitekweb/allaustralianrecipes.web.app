The ingredients needed to make Black bean cottage pie:
  1. Get 1 medium onion
  2. Prepare 1 stick celery
  3. Take 1 carrot
  4. Take 1 small leek
  5. Get 1 tsp chopped garlic
  6. Make ready 200 g cooked black beans (soak overnight and pressure cook 15 mins or cook 30 mins if dried)
  7. Take 1 tsp tomato puree
  8. Make ready 1 tsb bouillon in 1 cup of water
  9. Get Avocado oil
  10. Take 2 tbsps collagen/ bone broth (optional)
  11. Take 1 parsnip
  12. Take 2 small potatoes
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp goats butter
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup goats cheese/ parmesan
  15. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make Black bean cottage pie:
  1. Fry the carrot onion celery leek garlic in the avocado oil until beginning to soften. Add the bone broth and stock, cover and simmer until the carrots are tender.
  2. Add the black beans, black bean juice and tomato purée. Cook for 10 minutes then season.
  3. Boil the potatoes and parsnip. Drain and mash with butter.
  4. Put the bean mixture in a dish. Top with the potato mixture and grated cheese. Grill until the cheese melts and browns.
