The ingredients needed to make Broad beans Rice:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Cooked rice
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp Oil
  3. Take 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  5. Get 1 tsp Urad dal
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Channa dal
  7. Make ready 10 Curry leaves
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp Peanut
  9. Take 1 Onion Chopped finely
  10. Get 1 tsp Ginger chopped finely
  11. Make ready 1 Green chilli sliced
  12. Prepare 1 Tomato chopped finely
  13. Make ready 1 cup Broad beans sliced
  14. Make ready 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  15. Get Pinch Hing
  16. Make ready 2 tsp Sambar powder
  17. Make ready As needed Salt
  18. Prepare 2 tbsp grated coconut
  19. Make ready leaves To garnish Coriander
  20. Prepare 1 tsp Gingelly oil
