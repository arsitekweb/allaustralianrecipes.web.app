The ingredients needed to make Pancha Ratna Dosa:
  1. Prepare 2 cups rice(any variety)
  2. Get 2 cups toor dal
  3. Take 1/2 cup masoor dal
  4. Get 1/2 cup bellar dal (avarai parupu)
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup (2 tbsp: horesegram,Fenugreek seeds, broad beans)
  6. Get 1 thumb size ginger
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp jeera
  8. Make ready 4-5 green chillies as per spice tolerance

Instructions to make Pancha Ratna Dosa:
  1. Take a big container and add all the dal varieties
  2. Add water till brim of the vessel and soak the dal,ginger,green chillies, jeera over night
  3. At morning rinse the water. Note: please use a filter so that the jeera seeds ginger green chillies are not wasted
  4. Take a mixer and grind it into fine batter.Add salt and mix well.The mixture will be semi solid. It can be diluted with water as per your preference
