The ingredients needed to make Chiken tikka karahi:
  1. Get 1 chiken
  2. Make ready 1 medium bowl yougart
  3. Take 2 tsp salt
  4. Take 2 tbsp red chili powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp coriender powder
  6. Take 1 tsp haldi
  7. Get 1 tbsp garlic paste
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger paste
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp garam masala grind
  10. Make ready 1 tsp black paper
  11. Make ready 1 lemon/ fist ful coriender/garlic slice /green chili slice
  12. Make ready 1 cup oil
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp venigar
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp soya souce

Steps to make Chiken tikka karahi:
  1. Take All the ingredients according to recipie
  2. Add venigar soya souce n ginger garlic paste in chiken bowl n mix well then add all dry ingredients in yougart bowl n mix well
  3. Then add yougart mixture in chiken n mix well n let it rest for 30 to 50 mins
  4. Take karahi n add oil in it then add marinated chiken in hot oil
  5. Cover it with lid n let it cook on high flame for 10 to 12 mins
  6. After that take off the lid n cook it well untill it relise oil
  7. Take onioun peel n place buring charcol on it then drop oil on it n cover it with lid to add some bbq flavor
  8. Finaly add the garnishing ingdrients n sever on plate
