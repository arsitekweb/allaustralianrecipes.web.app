Steps to make Dragon Chicken with egg fried rice:
  1. Marinate the chicken and let rest for atleast 10 mins.
  2. Then deep fry the chicken on medium heat. Do not over fry.
  3. Keep the chicken aside. In a non stick wok, add oil and crushed garlic and saute.
  4. Add the onions and capsicum and saute for a minute. Then add the chicken and saute.
  5. Now add all the sauces gradually, mixing everything together.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve with egg fried rice.
