The ingredients needed to make Carrot(gajar)ka halwa:
  1. Make ready 500 gm gajar
  2. Make ready 500 gm milk
  3. Take 2 tbsp desi ghee
  4. Make ready 200 gm sugar
  5. Make ready 1 cup badam flakes
  6. Make ready 4 to 5 elaichi
  7. Prepare 1/4 khoya
  8. Make ready hand ful mixed nuts,pista,cashew and almonds

Steps to make Carrot(gajar)ka halwa:
  1. Wash carrot and Peel them and grate into food processor
  2. Mix carrot and milk a pan to a bowl and cook on low flame
  3. Add the ghee and cook 20 - 25 minutes
  4. Add sugar chopped cashew,nuts almonds and elaichi mix well.
  5. Stir cook till 5-10 minutes
  6. And transfer it to a serving bowl and garnish with sliced almonds.
