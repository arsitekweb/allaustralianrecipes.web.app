The ingredients needed to make Shukto:
  1. Prepare 1 cup raddish, diced
  2. Prepare 1 cup green plantain, diced
  3. Prepare 1 cup green papaya, diced
  4. Get 7-8 broad beans, chopped into half
  5. Get 1 cup yam, diced
  6. Take 1 small potato, diced
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup bitter gourd, sliced
  8. Prepare 2-3 tbsp mustard oil
  9. Get 6-7 bori (lentil dumplings / mangodi / vadi)
  10. Get 1 whole dry red chilli
  11. Take 1 tsp panch phoron (equal quantity of fennel, mustard, cumin, fenugreek & nigella seeds
  12. Prepare 2 bay leaves
  13. Prepare to taste salt
  14. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  15. Get 2 tbsp mustard paste / kasundi
  16. Get 1/2 cup milk
  17. Prepare 1 tsp ghee
  18. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar (opttional)

Steps to make Shukto:
  1. Heat 1 tbsp. oil in a pan and fry the bori till golden brown in colour. Drain and keep aside. In the same oil fry the sliced bitter gourd till light golden brown and keep aside.
  2. Heat the remaining of the oil and temper with the dry red chilli, bay leaves and the panch phoron.
  3. After it stops spluttering, add the chopped veggies and saute on low flame for 5-7 minutes till light brown. Add 11/2 cups water, fried bori, salt and turmeric powder.
  4. Cover and cook on low flame till the veggies turns soft and the gravy is slightly reduced. Now add the milk, ghee, sugar and mustard paste. Simmer for 2-3 minutes and serve with steamed rice.
