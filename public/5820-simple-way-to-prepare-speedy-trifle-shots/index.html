The ingredients needed to make Trifle shots:
  1. Get 1 vanilla cake mix.
  2. Get 10 pieces of strawberries. (add more if needed)
  3. Prepare 1 hand ful of mint leaves
  4. Take 6 pieces of passion fruit
  5. Make ready 1 cup Rose syrup
  6. Make ready 2 stick wild berries
  7. Get 2 can heavy cream
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 cup mascarbony cheese
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup whipping cream
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup full fat milk
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  12. Prepare 1/2 lemon juice

Steps to make Trifle shots:
  1. Do the cake mix as it says in the instructions
  2. Mix the fruit with the rose syrup and the mint
  3. Mix the mascarbony cheese with the lemon juice then gradually add the rest of the ingredients
  4. Cut the cake into cubes and put them as the first layer in your serving bowl.
  5. The fruit mix is your second layer.
  6. Lastly add the cream mix as the third layer.
