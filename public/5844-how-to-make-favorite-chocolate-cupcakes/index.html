The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cupcakes:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1/2 cup milk
  4. Take 1 1/2 cup flour
  5. Take Cooking chocolate (for presentation purpose)
  6. Take Colour ful sprinkles (for presentation purpose)
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence(optional)
  9. Make ready 100 gm butter (room temperature)
  10. Make ready 1 cup sugar (grounded)

Instructions to make Chocolate cupcakes:
  1. First take butter (room temp) and sugar and beat it together. Till it gets white in colour and creamy.
  2. Add 3 eggs one at a time and beat it well.
  3. Add vanilla essence (optional) and milk and beat again.
  4. Add flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder and mix it well with a spatula.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes at 180 degrees.
  6. If you are not sure that cupcakes are done or not, a pro tip to check is that insert any kind of stick in the centre of cupcakes and pull it back. If it comes back clean so your cupcakes are ready, if not so let it bake for few more minutes.
  7. Lay down cupcakes on a tray and let them cool.
  8. Melt the chocolate in microwave with a little cream. Pour the melted chocolate on your cupcakes and add some sprinkles.
