Steps to make Fried rice with beef and fish:
  1. Parboil rice, till it softens a bit. Set aside
  2. Dice all the vegetables and set aside
  3. Cook your fish and meat, adding salt onion and blended fresh pepper
  4. Set the stock aside, and parboil your rice
  5. Parboil for 20 minutes till it softens, wash the rice and set aside…
  6. Dice your vegetables, and Fry adding curry powder
  7. Fry for 10mins.
  8. Pour the fried vegetables into the rice and stir very well.
  9. Add the stock to the rice and allow for 5mins
  10. Serve hot and enjoy
