Steps to make Fava bean and Cheese salad:
  1. Prepare 2 lbs beans for salad by shelling the beans.
  2. Boil in lightly salted water for 1 to 2 minutes. Drain.
  3. Put beans in ice water.
  4. Remove the husks.
  5. Combine with thinly sliced cheese. Add lemon juice, olive oil and season to taste.
