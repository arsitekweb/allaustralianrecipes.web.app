Instructions to make Undhiyu:
  1. To make Muthiya, mix all Muthiya ingredients together. Make a hard dough. Take a small portion and give shape like Muthiya and fry on medium-low flame. Keep it ready.
  2. Soak all the dry ingredients for overnight or minimum 8 hours. Chop all vegetables into big pieces.
  3. Take a heavy bottom cooker. Add oil, crushed ginger garlic and green chillies and asfoitida. Saute for some time. Add red chilli powder turmeric powder and Garam masala
  4. Now add all soaked grams. Add vegetables.
  5. Add fried Muthiya. Salt and half glass water. Close the cooker and give 5-7 whistles. Let it cool. Open and mix it softly.
  6. Serve with Roti or Puri or Paratha
