The ingredients needed to make Kachnal with green chane:
  1. Make ready 200 gm Kachnal ke ful
  2. Prepare 200 gm green chane
  3. Prepare to taste Salt and red chilli powder
  4. Take 1 tsp haldi powder
  5. Get 1 tsp Dry coriander powder
  6. Get 1 tsp garam masala
  7. Make ready 3 finely chopped onion
  8. Make ready 3 finely chopped tomato
Instructions to make Kachnal with green chane:
  1. Soak overnight kachnal flower.
  2. Take a pan and dry roast onion and tomato.
  3. Add all spices and mix well.
  4. Add boil chane and boil kachnal.
  5. Cook for 5 minute and garnish with green Coriander.
  6. Serve hot with chapati.
