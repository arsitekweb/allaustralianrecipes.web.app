The ingredients needed to make Bauk Fuler Bora:
  1. Prepare 8 pieces bauk ful
  2. Make ready 500 gm besan
  3. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp nigella seed
  5. Make ready As needed salt
  6. Get As needed turmeric powder
  7. Take 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Prepare As needed refine oil

Steps to make Bauk Fuler Bora:
  1. Take a bowl and put all the ingredients except bauk ful
  2. Pour little water in that bowl and make a thick batter
  3. Now, take a pan and put refine oil as needed for deep fry
  4. When the oil become very hot, take the bauk ful, dip in that batter one by one and deep fry.
