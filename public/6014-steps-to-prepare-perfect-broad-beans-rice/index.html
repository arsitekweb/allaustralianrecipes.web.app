The ingredients needed to make Broad beans Rice:
  1. Get 1 cup Cooked rice
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp Oil
  3. Take 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  4. Take 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  5. Take 1 tsp Urad dal
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Channa dal
  7. Make ready 10 Curry leaves
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp Peanut
  9. Take 1 Onion Chopped finely
  10. Get 1 tsp Ginger chopped finely
  11. Prepare 1 Green chilli sliced
  12. Make ready 1 Tomato chopped finely
  13. Prepare 1 cup Broad beans sliced
  14. Make ready 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  15. Prepare Pinch Hing
  16. Take 2 tsp Sambar powder
  17. Make ready As needed Salt
  18. Make ready 2 tbsp grated coconut
  19. Make ready leaves To garnish Coriander
  20. Take 1 tsp Gingelly oil

  1. Heat wide pan add oil. Once hot add mustard seeds, cumin seeds. Once mustard splutter add urad dal, channal dal. Once channa dal slightly roasted add curry leaves and peanuts.
