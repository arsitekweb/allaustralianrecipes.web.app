The ingredients needed to make Egyptian style Falafel:
  1. Make ready 2 cups (300 g) split fava beans
  2. Take 1 cup finely chopped(dill, parsley and coriander)
  3. Get 1 large sized onion (or chive) and 4 cloves of garlic
  4. Prepare 1 flat tablespoon of salt
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon black pepper
  6. Get 2 teaspoons each (cumin and ground coriander)
