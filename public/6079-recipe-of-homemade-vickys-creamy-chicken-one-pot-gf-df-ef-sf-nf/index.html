Steps to make Vickys Creamy Chicken One-Pot, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. My recipe above for seasoned flour makes more than you'll need here but it's great for frying wings etc so I keep a big container of it in my cupboard all the time
  2. Toss the chicken in the flour then brown off in a pan with the oil over a medium heat
  3. Remove the chicken and fry off the bacon in the pan until crisp then put the chicken back in
  4. Add the stock and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Add in the potatoes and simmer covered for a further 20 minutes
  6. Stir in the beans, sweetcorn and tomatoes. Simmer uncovered for a final 5 minutes
  7. Remove the chicken and plate up.
