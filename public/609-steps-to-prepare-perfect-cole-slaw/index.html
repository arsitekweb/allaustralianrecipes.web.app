The ingredients needed to make Cole Slaw:
  1. Take 1/2 head cabbage, chopped
  2. Get 1 carrot, shredded
  3. Take 1 celery or kohlrabi, shredded (optional)
  4. Get 1 parsnip, shredded (optional)
  5. Get 1/2 cup vinegar
  6. Take 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  7. Take 1 1/2 cup mayonnaise

Steps to make Cole Slaw:
  1. In a large bowl, toss cabbage with additional vegetables.
  2. In a small bowl, blend vinegar and mayonnaise.
  3. Pour vinegar mixture over cabbage and toss until coated.
  4. Add mayonnaise to taste, approximately 1 1/2 cups.
