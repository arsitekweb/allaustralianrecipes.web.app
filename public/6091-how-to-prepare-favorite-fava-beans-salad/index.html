The ingredients needed to make Fava beans salad:
  1. Prepare 2 cans fava beans
  2. Get 1 cup yogurt
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup tahini

  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon hot paprika
  7. Get 1 tablespoon cumin
  8. Take 4 minced cloves garlic
  9. Make ready 3 tablespoon diced tomatoes
  10. Get 1 tablespoon parsley
  11. Take Chopped green onion and pomegranate seed and dry mint optional
