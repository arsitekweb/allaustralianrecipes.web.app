The ingredients needed to make Fava bean and Cheese salad:
  1. Prepare 2 cup fava beans (yield from 2 lbs fresh)
  2. Take 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  3. Take 2 tbsp cheese thinly sliced
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  5. Get 1 pinch red pepper flakes

Steps to make Fava bean and Cheese salad:
  1. Prepare 2 lbs beans for salad by shelling the beans.
  2. Boil in lightly salted water for 1 to 2 minutes. Drain.
  3. Put beans in ice water.
  4. Remove the husks.
  5. Combine with thinly sliced cheese. Add lemon juice, olive oil and season to taste.
