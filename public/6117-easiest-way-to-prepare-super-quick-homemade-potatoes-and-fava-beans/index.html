The ingredients needed to make Potatoes and fava beans:
  1. Prepare 6 medium potatoes
  2. Prepare 1 small packet of fava beans
  3. Take 1 cup milk
  4. Take 2 cups water
  5. Make ready 1 tablespoon thick cream
  6. Take 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon mixed herbs
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon garlic flakes
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon white pepper
  10. Take 1 cube onion and green pepper

Steps to make Potatoes and fava beans:
