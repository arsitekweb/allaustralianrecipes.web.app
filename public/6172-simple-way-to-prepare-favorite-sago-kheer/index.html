The ingredients needed to make Sago Kheer:
  1. Take 750 ml ful cream milk
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup sago
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp cream
  4. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped almonds
  6. Make ready few raisins
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla essence

Steps to make Sago Kheer:
  1. Wash and soak sago for 2 hours.
  2. Heat milk in a pan, boil for 5 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Add sago, boil and cook on low flame for 10 minutes, add sugar. Mix and add cream.
  4. Add chopped almond and raisins. Adjust consistency of kheer. Remove from flame.
  5. Add vanilla essence, mix and dish out in serving bowls.
