The ingredients needed to make Dalma:
  1. Take 1/2 cup Tuvar dal (split pigeon pea lentil), soaked for 30 minutes
  2. Make ready 3-4 cups chopped mixed veggies (bottle gourd, beans, pumpkin, raw papaya, eggplant, raw banana, broad beans, cauliflower, potato, radish)
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp. mustard oil
  4. Get 1 tsp. panch phoron
  5. Get 2 bay leaves
  6. Get 2 dry red chilies
  7. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  8. Take 1 tsp. ginger, chopped
  9. Prepare To taste salt
  10. Take 1 tsp. cumin powder
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp. turmeric powder
  12. Prepare 1 tsp. chilli powder
  13. Prepare 1 tsp. garam masala powder
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp. ghee
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp. fresh grated coconut
  16. Take 1 tsp. coriander leaves, chopped

Steps to make Dalma:
  1. Cut all the veggies and keep aside.
  2. Pressure cook the dal along with all the chopped veggie & water for 2 whistles. Keep aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan & temper with bay leaves, dry red chilies & panch phoron.
  4. After it stops spluttering, add the onion, ginger and turmeric powder. - Saute till light brown.
  5. Add the boiled dal, salt,
  6. Cumin powder, garam masala powder and red chilli powder. Cover and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  7. When done, add ghee and mix well.
  8. Garnish with fresh grated coconut & coriander leaves. Serve with hot - steamed rice.
