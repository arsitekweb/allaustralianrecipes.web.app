  9. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon cumin powder
  10. Get 1/2 tablespoon salt

Steps to make The Green Stew:
  1. Cut all the vegetables and make ready
  2. Heat a pan with oil and temper fenugreek seeds. Add the chopped vegetables except the spinach. Sauté with salt and turmeric powder.
  3. Sauté till the vegetables are little soft then add ginger, cumin coriander powder and continue sautéing for few more minutes
  4. Add the spinach in it
  5. Sauté for a minute again
  6. Add water and boil till the reduces to half and vegetables are cooked
  7. The leafy Stew is ready to serve with some chopped coriander leaves.
