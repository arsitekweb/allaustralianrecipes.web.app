The ingredients needed to make Fish Stew:
  1. Get 1 fresh crocker fish
  2. Make ready 4 fresh tomatoes big
  3. Take 4 fresh pepper
  4. Take 1 bulb onions
  5. Prepare 1 hand ful scent leaf diced
  6. Take 2 knorr cube
  7. Make ready to taste salt
  8. Prepare 1 cksp veg oil

Instructions to make Fish Stew:
  1. Wash tomatoes, pepper and onions and blend not too smooth and set it aside, wash fish and spice to your taste and fry; set aside afterward
  2. Heat up small pot and add vegetable oil, sprinkle a little salt into the oil once it hot and pour in the blended tomatoes paste
  3. After 2 minutes, add the fried fish and allow everything simmer without stiring it,
  4. Once the water in the tomatoes is dried up and the oil is floating start stiring in the same process mash the fish and de bone in the process
  5. Add salt and knorr cube and finally add the scent leaf and turn off the heat and serve with white rice or boiled yam
