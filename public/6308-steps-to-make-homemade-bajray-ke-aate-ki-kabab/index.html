The ingredients needed to make Bajray ke Aate ki Kabab:
  1. Make ready 1/2 kg Beef mince
  2. Prepare 100 g Hara lehsan
  3. Make ready 1.5 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  4. Take 1 tsp Green chilli paste
  5. Get 1 tsp Crushed red chilli
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  8. Take 1 tsp Cumin
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Dry crushed coriander
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  11. Prepare Salt as required
  12. Take 2 Onions (thinly sliced)
  13. Get 1 Tomato cut in cubes
  14. Take Coriander leaves….hand ful
  15. Prepare 6 tbsp Bajre ka aata
  16. Make ready 1 Egg for binding

Instructions to make Bajray ke Aate ki Kabab:
  1. Mix all the ingredients in beef mince except aata.Mince should be dry. Mix very well.
  2. Now add bajre ka aata and egg and again mix well.
  3. Make balls and deep fry on medium low heat till golden brown.
  4. Serve with ketchup and raita.
