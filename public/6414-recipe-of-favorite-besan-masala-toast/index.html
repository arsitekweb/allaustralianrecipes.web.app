The ingredients needed to make Besan masala toast:
  1. Make ready 8-10 bread slices
  2. Take 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  3. Get 1/2 cup curd
  4. Make ready 1 small capsicum finely chopped
  5. Take 1 tomato finely chopped
  6. Make ready 1 onion finely chopped
  7. Get to taste salt
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  10. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  11. Get 1 green chilli finely chopped
  12. Make ready hand ful of fresh coriander
  13. Get pinch baking soda

Instructions to make Besan masala toast:
  1. In a bowl add gram flour and curd mix well add little water and to make batter.
  2. Now add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, salt and mix well.
  3. Now add finely chopped onion, capsicum, tomato, green chilli and fresh coriander, mix everything well. - Add baking soda,mix well keep aside.
  4. Now heat a pan and add little oil into it. - Now take a bread slice, apply green chutney on one side, take another slice of bread, apply tomato sauce on it and put it on one another.
  5. Now spread prepared mix of besan mixture on one side and place upside down in the heated pan.
  6. Let it roast for a min and spread besan masala mixture on the top side too and carefully filp it. let it roast from another side too on low flame till nice crisp.
  7. Besan masala toast is ready, cut it into two halves and serve.
