The ingredients needed to make Phulkopir Data Chorchori/ Cauliflower Stalk Curry:
  1. Get 1/2 cup cubed radish
  2. Make ready 1 potato cubed
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup broad beans (shim)
  4. Take 1 tbsp chopped string beans
  5. Get 7-8 pieces eggplant cubed
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp coriander chopped
  7. Take 4-5 cauliflower florets chopped
  8. Take 4-5 cauliflower stems sliced thinly
  9. Get 1 tbsp mustard paste
  10. Get 2 green chillies
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  12. Take 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp paanch phoron
  14. Take To taste salt
  15. Get 1 tsp sugar
  16. Make ready 2.5 tbsp mustard oil
