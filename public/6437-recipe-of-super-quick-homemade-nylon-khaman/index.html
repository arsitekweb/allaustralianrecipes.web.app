The ingredients needed to make Nylon khaman:
  1. Make ready 1 cup besan
  2. Get 1 cup water
  3. Take 1-1/2 tbsp sooji
  4. Take 4 tsp sugar
  5. Take 1/4 tsp citric acid (Nimbu ke ful)
  6. Get 1/4 tsp ground green chillies
  7. Get 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  8. Make ready 1 tiny pinch baking soda
  9. Make ready 1 tsp eno (fruits salt)
  10. Make ready For tadka…
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  12. Take 2 tsp rai
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  14. Prepare 3 green chillies chopped
  15. Prepare as needed Curry leaves
  16. Make ready 1 tsp white sesame seeds
  17. Prepare 1/2 cup water

Instructions to make Nylon khaman:
  1. In a mixing bowl mix all indgridients except eno and rest for 10 mins.
  2. Add eno and steam for 15 to 20 mins.
  3. When khaman is done Let it come to room temperature then temper.(tadka)
  4. To make tadka heat oil,add rai when it crackles add green chillies.
  5. Then add white sesame seeds and water.
  6. Add sugar and curry leaves.
  7. Let it boil till water reduces to half then pour this tadka over khaman pieces.
  8. Serve with coriander leaves and grated fresh coconut.
