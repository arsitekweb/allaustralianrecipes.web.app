The ingredients needed to make Peri peri bites:
  1. Take 12 green chillies big in size
  2. Get 12 cooked chickn strips
  3. Make ready 12 chadder chese strips
  4. Get 4 tas cream
  5. Get 2 tas vinegar
  6. Get 1 bowl ful of water
  7. Prepare Flour for coating
  8. Prepare Egg for coating
  9. Prepare Bread crums for coating

Add black pepper, peri peri sauce, lemon juice, soya sauce and vinegar. Give slit on green chilies and remove the seeds. Mix all the ingredients together including grilled chicken for filling in bowl. Dip in prepared batter, roll twice in bread crumbs.

Steps to make Peri peri bites:
  1. Cut the chillies from the center and remove all the seeds from it
  2. Mix water and vinegar in a bowl and add the chillies in to it and leave it for 3omins
  3. Cook chicken strips with any of favourite method
  4. Take out chillies from water and dry them properly using tissue paper
  5. Fill the chillies first by adding chicken strip then cream and thn cheese. Fill all other chillies by using the same process
  6. With soft hands close the opening of chillies so the strip may not drop outside.
  7. 1st coat the chillies with flour then edd then crums. Repeat coating of egg and crums.
  8. Cold temperature oil will damage the coating so kindly heat the oil properly before frying
