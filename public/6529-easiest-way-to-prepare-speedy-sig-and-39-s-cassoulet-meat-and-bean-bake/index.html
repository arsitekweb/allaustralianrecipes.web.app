The ingredients needed to make sig's Cassoulet ( Meat and Bean Bake):

  2. Make ready 2 large onions finely chopped
  3. Make ready 1 pinch each salt and pepper
  4. Make ready 3 cloves
  5. Take 1 bayleaf
