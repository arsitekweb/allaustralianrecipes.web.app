The ingredients needed to make Creamy bean:
  1. Make ready 1 bag frozen green fava beans
  2. Make ready 1 pound chopped lamb shanks
  3. Prepare 6 cup hot water
  4. Make ready 2 onions
  5. Prepare bag Spice
  6. Take 5 garlic
  7. Make ready 2 cups yoghurt
  8. Prepare 1 Yolk
  9. Make ready 2 tablespoon cornstarch
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon Salt
  11. Take 1 teaspoon peper
  12. Make ready 1 teaspoon turmeric
  13. Prepare 1 teaspoon cumin
  14. Make ready 4 cups meat broth
  15. Make ready 4 tablespoon oil
