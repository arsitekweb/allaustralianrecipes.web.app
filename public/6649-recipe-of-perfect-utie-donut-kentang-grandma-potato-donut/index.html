The ingredients needed to make Utie Donut Kentang (Grandma Potato Donut):
  1. Get 500 gram plain flour (400 high protein and 100 normal)
  2. Take 50 gram powder milk
  3. Make ready 1 sachet instan yest (10 gram)
  4. Prepare 200 gram steam potato (mashed)
  5. Prepare 100 gram caster sugar
  6. Get 75 gram margarine
  7. Get 2 eggs
  8. Prepare 100 ml ful cream milk or water

Steps to make Utie Donut Kentang (Grandma Potato Donut):
  1. Mix flour, milk powder, sugar and yeast then stir until evenly distributed. - Add mashed and cooled potatoes, eggs and add liquid milk / water gradually knead until smooth and smooth. - Then add salt and butter, then knead again until they are completely smooth and elastic.
  2. After it is smooth, leave it for about 30 minutes then divide the dough into 65 grams each and round it. - After being left let stand again until fluffy. After expanding the middle hole. Or shape according to taste. - Then fry the donuts until browned.
