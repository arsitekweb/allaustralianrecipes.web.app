The ingredients needed to make Fava Bean Spread on a Flat Bread with Prosciutto:
  1. Make ready 2 cup fresh or frozen fava beans (I used frozen)
  2. Take 1/2 small red or green apple core taken out
  3. Make ready 1 small lemon (get 2 tsp zest & 1 tblsp juice from it)
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp olive oil
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp prepared mustard
  6. Prepare 8 slice of flatbread
  7. Make ready 8 slice prosciutto (thin slices of ham, roast beef, pastrami, or smoked salmon can be used for variation)

Steps to make Fava Bean Spread on a Flat Bread with Prosciutto:
  1. boil beans in water for 3 minutes until tender but still firm
  2. rinse in cold water
  3. in a blender pulse beans, oil, lemon zest & juice, half apple and mustard till mixture is creamy but chunky
  4. spread fava bean mixture onto flatbread, top with slice of prosciutto or topping of choice
