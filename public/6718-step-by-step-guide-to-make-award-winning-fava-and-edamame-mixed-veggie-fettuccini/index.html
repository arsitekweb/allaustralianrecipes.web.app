Steps to make Fava and Edamame Mixed Veggie Fettuccini:

  2. while pasta is boiling cut up zucchini, onion, broccoli, in course cuts
  3. fry Bacon in heavy skillet. drain Bacon on paper towel.
  4. turn the fire to medium and add ALL the veggies. coat with Bacon drippings.. onion should be soft but the veggies are soft crunchy …
