The ingredients needed to make Broad beans thuvaiyal:
  1. Take 250 gms Broad beans
  2. Get 15 nos Small onion
  3. Prepare 3 Green chillies
  4. Make ready Handful Curry leaves
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
  6. Make ready 2 tablespoons Oil
  7. Take 1 tablespoon Coriander seeds
  8. Take 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds
  9. Make ready 1 tablespoon Pepper
  10. Make ready 1 tablespoon Split bengal grams
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup Coconut
  12. Make ready As per taste Salt
  13. Get As per taste Water
