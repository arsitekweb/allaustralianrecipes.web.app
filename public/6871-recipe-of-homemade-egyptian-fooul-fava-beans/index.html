The ingredients needed to make Egyptian Fooul (Fava beans):
  1. Make ready 16 oz Fava Bean can
  2. Take 3 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  3. Get 1/2 cup water
  4. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  5. Make ready 1 tsp paprika powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp ground cumin
  7. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  9. Take 1 tsp oregano

Steps to make Egyptian Fooul (Fava beans):
