The ingredients needed to make Jake's fava fawai (A steamed fava beans knockout):
  1. Get 300 grams Fava beans
  2. Prepare 5 green chillies
  3. Take 3/4 cup water
  4. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Take 1 dash salt (or as per ur taste)
  6. Take 1 tbsp Oil
  7. Take 1 few curry leaves

Steps to make Jake's fava fawai (A steamed fava beans knockout):
  1. cut the pointed sides of Beans n remove the string off the sides
  2. slit the chillies into half, which makes it 10 pieces
  3. cut the beans into small pieces
  4. In a pan, add the beans, chillies and water and heat it on a cooking stove
  5. Add salt and turmeric powder and stir it well.
  6. Close it with a lid and let it cook until the water dry up. But make sure you stir it occassionally so that all sides get cooked well.
  7. Add curry leaves and oil to it and stir well.
  8. Let it heat on low flame for 5 min and saute in between
  9. off the flame, cover it with a lid and let it stay under the steam for 5 min before serving.
