The ingredients needed to make Broad bean seed dal:
  1. Take 200 grams Dry seeds of broad beans
  2. Make ready 10-12 flakes of garlic
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp green coriander leaves
  4. Prepare 1 tomato finely chopped
  5. Take 2 tbsp Mustard oil
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp panchforan
  7. Prepare 4 whole dry red chillies
  8. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp chilli powder
  10. Prepare as per taste Salt

Instructions to make Broad bean seed dal:
  1. First dry roast dried broad bean seeds you have to roast carefully these seeds.
  2. After roasting removes upper skins from the seeds & clean well.
  3. Now wash with water & keep in cooker with 3 cups water & with salt & turmeric & cook till 6-7 whistles come.
  4. Now the seeds are softened & dal cooked well.
  5. Now mix the dal with a ladle well.
  6. Now take a tadka pan heat well add oil then add panch foran & dried red chillis then add chopped garlic then add chopped tomatoes fry well all ingredients when tomatoes became soften then add red chilli powder then add coriander leaves & then mix the hot tadka mixture into dal & mix it well.
  7. At last add coriander leaves to the dal & serve with rice.
