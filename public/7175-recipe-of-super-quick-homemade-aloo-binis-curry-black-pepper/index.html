The ingredients needed to make Aloo binis curry (black pepper):
  1. Get 100 grams binis phalli
  2. Get 1 medium sized potato
  3. Prepare 2 medium sized onions
  4. Take 1 large tomato
  5. Get As required Oil for cooking
  6. Take 1 teaspoon curry powder
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek leaves
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon Ginger garlic paste
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon Salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon heap ful black pepper powder
  12. Take Pinch garam masala

Steps to make Aloo binis curry (black pepper):
  1. Chop all vegetables, keep aside and cut the potatoes into round-shaped, beans 1 inch in size and keep it in water. Now take the onions and tomatoes and chopped it.
  2. Then in a pan add tomatoes, onions, dry spices, oil and let in cook for 15 minutes covered.
  3. Then fry it till the oil separates from it then add a cup of water and add potatoes and beans then again cook for more 10-15 minutes.
  4. It’s ready serve it hot with rice or chapati.
