The ingredients needed to make Fava Beans, Shrimp, and Scallops in a Basil Mayonnaise Dressing:
  1. Prepare 200 grams Fava beans
  2. Prepare 10 Shrimp (large)
  3. Take 1 can Scallops (canned)
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
  5. Take 1 Dried basil
  6. Get 1 Salt and pepper

Steps to make Fava Beans, Shrimp, and Scallops in a Basil Mayonnaise Dressing:
  1. Boil the fava beans, but not too much (about 5 minutes) and remove the membranes. If you over-boil them, they will become discolored, so adjust the boiling time as needed.
  2. Prepare and boil the shrimp. While they're boiling, add a bit of sake to the pot. Use any size shrimp you like; even small ones are okay.
  3. Combine the fava beans, scallops, shrimp, mayonnaise, basil, and salt. Add pepper for an even more delicious touch.
