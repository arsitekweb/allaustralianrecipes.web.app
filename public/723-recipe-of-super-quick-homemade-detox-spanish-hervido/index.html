The ingredients needed to make Detox Spanish hervido:
  1. Make ready 2 large carrots
  2. Take 1 parsnip
  3. Take 1 large onion
  4. Prepare 2 potatoes
  5. Take 1 handful green beens
  6. Prepare 1 handful mangetout
  7. Make ready 3 artichokes
  8. Take 1 bayleaf
  9. Make ready Olive oil
  10. Take Salt

Instructions to make Detox Spanish hervido:
  1. Bring a pan of water to boiling point and add the bayleaf, a generous pinch of salt and a dash of olive oil. Peel, wash and cut the potatoes, carrots and parsnip, cover and leave to simmer for 10 mins
  2. Cut the onion and peel and halve the artichokes. Add to the pan and cover again. Leave for another 10 mins.
  3. Wash the beans and mangetout and add. Cover for a final 10 mins. Warm a serving dish in the oven.
  4. Drain and arrange in the serving dish
  5. Pick out what you want to eat, drizzle with olive oil and salt (or soy sauce) and enjoy!!
