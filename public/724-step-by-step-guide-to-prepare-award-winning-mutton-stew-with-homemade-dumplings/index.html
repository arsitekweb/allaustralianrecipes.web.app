The ingredients needed to make Mutton stew with homemade dumplings:
  1. Make ready 1/2 kilo mutton on the bone
  2. Take 1 onion, sliced
  3. Get 4 tbs plain flour*
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt*
  5. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper*
  6. Make ready 1-2 tsp dried herbs (i.e. rosemary, mixed herbs)*
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup pearl barley
  8. Make ready 2 large carrots, chopped
  9. Get 2 parsnips, peeled and chopped
  10. Take 2 medium potatoes, quartered
  11. Make ready 1 broccoli trunk, chopped (the tough base part) optional
  12. Get 1/2 cup gravy granules mixed with 1 cup boiling water
  13. Take dumplings**
  14. Take 1 1/2 cups self raising flour
  15. Take 1/2 TSP salt
  16. Make ready 3 TBS olive oil
  17. Prepare Optional: ½tsp dried herbs rosemary/mixed herbs
  18. Get Milk (approx ½ cup or so)

Instructions to make Mutton stew with homemade dumplings:
  1. Brown onions in frying pan. Add to Crockpot.
  2. Mix mutton with flour and starred (*) ingredients. Pan fry meat in batches, till browned. Add to Crockpot.
  3. Now add pearl barley, and veg, plus enough water to just cover everything. Set Crockpot on high for 4 hours or until meat and veg is tender. Stir in the gravy. Add additional salt/ pepper/herbs if needed.
  4. DUMPLINGS: First mix flour with salt and herbs. Then drizzle in the olive oil, and mix till it looks loose and crumbly. Then carefully add just enough milk to make a soft dough. Do not over-mix. Take spoonfuls and drop into the bubbling stew and cook for 20mins till double in size.
