Steps to make Future is Green:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C/Gas mark 6/400 degrees C.
  2. Boil the fava (broad) beans for 3 minutes, cool and shell.
  3. Place the fava beans in a food processor with the cottage cheese, skyr, avocado, jumbo oats and egg yolk and whizz until smooth.
  4. Whisk the egg white until stiff and fold gently into the fava bean mixture.
  5. Place small swirls of the mixture on a greased baking sheet and bake for 15 - 20 minutes until brown.
  6. Whilst the hot cakes are cooking, blitz the dressing ingredients.
  7. Serve the hot cakes with baby salad leaves and herbs, green tomato and drizzle with the tahini dressing.
