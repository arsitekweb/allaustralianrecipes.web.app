The ingredients needed to make Rice with edamame and dill - vegan:
  1. Make ready 1 cup rice
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Take Sea salt
  4. Get 2 cups - maybe more - water
  5. Get 1.5 cups edamame/ broad beans/ peas, shelled - fresh or frozen
  6. Get 1 large handful of dill
  7. Get Juice of 1/2 lemon

Buttery rice, permeating with the flavor of fresh dill and edamame. This tasty rice is served alongside chicken and meat shish kabobs. However, it's also a perfect vegetarian dish as well! Drizzle with vinaigrette just before serving.

Steps to make Rice with edamame and dill - vegan:
  1. In a pan with a lid, put the rice, oil, water and generous pinch of salt.
  2. Cover the pan, bring to the boil and simmer until most of the liquid is absorbed and the rice is almost cooked. The amount of simmering time the rice needs depends on the type of rice. It can be 20-30 mins.
  3. Add the edamame and dill. Stir then cover again and simmer for 5 mins.
