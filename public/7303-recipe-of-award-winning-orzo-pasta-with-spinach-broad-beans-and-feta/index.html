The ingredients needed to make Orzo pasta with spinach, Broad beans and feta:
  1. Take 1 garlic clove
  2. Make ready 300 g Greek youghurt
  3. Get 4 tablespoons olive oil
  4. Get 1 lemon
  5. Take 250 g orzo pasta
  6. Prepare 300 g frozen broad beans
  7. Get 150 g asparagus
  8. Prepare 100 g baby leaf spinach
  9. Take 1 handful fresh basil
  10. Prepare 200 g feta
