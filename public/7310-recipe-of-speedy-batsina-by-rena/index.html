The ingredients needed to make Batsina by Rena:
  1. Make ready half a small yellow aquash (about 300 g to 500 g or 2 ful handfuls) grated and thoroughly strained
  2. Prepare 4 eggs
  3. Make ready 1/2 kg feta cheese and 1/2 cup grated graviera (traditional Greek cheese) from Crete or any other spicy cheese
  4. Prepare 50 g -100 g butter (we suggest clarified or stakovoutiro -butter that comes from staka, a traditional Cretan dairy product) depending on what you like
  5. Prepare self-rising flour
  6. Get fresh milk
  7. Make ready salt & pepper

Instructions to make Batsina by Rena:
  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl, add the grated squash and mix.
  2. Add the grated cheeses (it is best to cut the feta in slightly larger pieces) and mix.
  3. Heat the butter lightly and add to the mixture.
  4. Since your goal is to make a batter that is not too thick (you should aim for the consistency of a thick omelette mixture) eyeball the rest of the ingredientsadd a little milk (let's say 1 cup) and mix adding the flour little by little (about one handful). Make sure that the batter is not too thick, if you realise that you have used too much flour, add some more milk. If the batter is too runny, add a little more flour.
  5. Season with salt and pepper (I used plenty of pepper and very little salt due to the cheeses).
  6. Empty the mixture in a baking tray that you have lightly brushed with oil. You should aim for a thin layer of batter.
  7. Bake on the bottom rack at 200°C until golden brown.
  8. Let it cool so you can cut it easily.
