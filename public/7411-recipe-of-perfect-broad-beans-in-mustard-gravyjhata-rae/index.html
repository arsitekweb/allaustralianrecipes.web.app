The ingredients needed to make Broad beans in mustard gravy(Jhata Rae):
  1. Take 200 grams broad beans
  2. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  3. Take 1 tomato just cut into 4
  4. Get as per taste Salt
  5. Take 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  7. Get pinch hing
  8. Take 1 red chilli
  9. Make ready For mustard gravy-
  10. Take 2 teaspoons mustard
  11. Get 1 onion
  12. Take 2 green chillies
  13. Take 1/2 tsp jeera powder
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp dhaniya
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  16. Take 5 Pods garlic
