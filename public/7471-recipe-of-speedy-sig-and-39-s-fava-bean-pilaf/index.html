The ingredients needed to make sig's Fava Bean Pilaf:
  1. Take 50 ml vegetable oil
  2. Make ready 1 onion finely chopped
  3. Make ready 2 gloves garlic, finely.chopped or.crushed
  4. Prepare 2 tsp each,cumin and coriander seeds ,dry toaated for a few seconds and crushed with,mortar and pestle
  5. Make ready 10 cardamon pods
  6. Prepare 2 cloves
  7. Get 2 inch / 5 cm cinnamon stick
  8. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric
  9. Take 225 grams basmati rice , washed in cold water for 20 minutes, drained,
  10. Make ready 200 grams frozen or fresh fava ( broad) beans , remove membrane .
  11. Get 400 ml vegetable stock
  12. Take 50 grams butter
  13. Prepare 4 zucchini, ,cut into 1 inch slices,
  14. Take 1 pinch each salt and pepper
  15. Take 6 tsp creme fraiche

Instructions to make sig's Fava Bean Pilaf:
  1. fry garlic and.onion in oil for 6-8 minutes until soft
  2. add all spices ,cook, stirring for 3 minutes,
  3. add beans ,rice.and stock, cover,cook over low heat to simmer for 12-15 minutes until rice is,tender,
  4. stir in the butter.
  5. meanwhile brush the sliced zucchini from both sides with oil and season,with salt and pepper,add to a hot griddle pan and cook for 3 minutes from each side until slightly charred
  6. serve the rice with the zucchini and garnish with creme fraiche
