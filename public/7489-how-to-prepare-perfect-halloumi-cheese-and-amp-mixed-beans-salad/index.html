  1. Make ready 100 Grams Halloumi Cheese

  13. Get 1 Teaspoon lemon juice
  14. Take 1 Teaspoon black pepper freshly cracked

Steps to make Halloumi cheese & mixed beans salad:
  1. Soak all the beans for 8 hrs and pressure cook them for 5-7 mins or 2 whistles only.
  2. Cut the halloumi cheese into 2 inch long strips, add little olive oil on a pan and grill the halloumi cheese from both sides. keep aside
  3. In a deep bowl mix all the beans & slice onions.
  4. Whisk the mustard powder with little water for 2 mins, it should become light and color should change to white. This will be used in the dressing
  5. Prepare dressing by mixing all the ingredients (olive oil, Honey, Lemon juice, mustard, freshly cracked pepper &Salt). Mix the dressing with the help of a whisk for a minute.
  6. Add this dressing in the beans & onions.Toss the salad Garnish with sesame seeds which are dry roasted Serve chilled.
