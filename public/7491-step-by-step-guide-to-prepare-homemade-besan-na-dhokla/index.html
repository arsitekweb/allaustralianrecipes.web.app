The ingredients needed to make Besan na dhokla:
  1. Make ready 200 gms besan
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp bati (mota wheat flour)
  3. Make ready as per taste Salt
  4. Take 2 tbsp chilli powder
  5. Get 1 tsp asafeotida
  6. Make ready 1 tsp crushed ajwain
  7. Get 1 tsp powdered sugar
  8. Take 1/2 tsp sajina ful
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp oil

Instructions to make Besan na dhokla:
  1. Mix all the dry ingredients and make a thick dough.
  2. Now make small small bhakhri and roast it on specially iron tawa. You can put 6 to 7 together like a tikki.
  3. Roast it with slow flame and turned it. Now roast a dhokla on jali like bengan roast in bengan bhartha.
  4. With the help of fingers make four holes on dhokla and dip it into oil.
  5. At least for 10 to 15 min dip the dhokla into oil, after that transfer the dhokla into storage container. You can keep it out side for 5 to 6 days.
