The ingredients needed to make Bigilla:
  1. Make ready 2 can broad bean/lima bean
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp crushed garlic
  3. Take 1 pinch ground cloves
  4. Prepare 3/4 tsp salt
  5. Get 4 tbsp olive oil
  6. Take 2 tsp hot sauce
  7. Make ready 5 tbsp chopped parsley
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup water
