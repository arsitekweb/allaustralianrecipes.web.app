The ingredients needed to make Broad Bean Hummus:
  1. Make ready 10-15 broad beans- split and beans removed
  2. Take Tin on chickpeas in water
  3. Take 2 tbsp tahini
  4. Prepare 3 cloves garlic
  5. Prepare Juice of 1 lemon
  6. Prepare Salt
  7. Make ready Olive oil
  8. Take Paprika

Instructions to make Broad Bean Hummus:
  1. Boil a saucepan of water.
  2. Pod all the beans and boil in the water for 5 minutes. Drain
  3. In a food processor throw in the cooked beans, drained chickpeas, garlic, lemon, tahini and 6 tbsp water.
  4. Blend until smooth. Salt to taste.
  5. Spoon into serving bowl, smooth over. Sprinkle with paprika and a generous glug of olive oil.
