The ingredients needed to make Broad beans giahni:
  1. Prepare 1/2 dried broad beans
  2. Take 1/2 cup oil
  3. Get 1 tbsp tomato paste
  4. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  5. Prepare 2 bay leaves
  6. Make ready 2 tomatoes, grated
  7. Get 1 onion finely chopped
  8. Take 2 cups vegetable stock
  9. Make ready salt
  10. Take pepper

Instructions to make Broad beans giahni:
  1. Soak the broad beans overnight.
  2. On the next day, rinse them and boil them in a pot with plenty of water for 15 minutes.
  3. Strain the broad beans and use the same pot to sauté the onion in the oil.
  4. Add the broad beans, the tomato paste dissolved in the stock, the tomato, the sugar, the pepper and the bay leaves.
  5. Mix well and let it cook over medium heat until the broad beans are done (about 45 minutes).
  6. A little while before you remove from heat, add the salt.
