The ingredients needed to make Utie Donut Kentang (Grandma Potato Donut):
  1. Prepare 500 gram plain flour (400 high protein and 100 normal)
  2. Make ready 50 gram powder milk
  3. Get 1 sachet instan yest (10 gram)
  4. Make ready 200 gram steam potato (mashed)
  5. Take 100 gram caster sugar
  6. Get 75 gram margarine
  7. Get 2 eggs
  8. Make ready 100 ml ful cream milk or water
