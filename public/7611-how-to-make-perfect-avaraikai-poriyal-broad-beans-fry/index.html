The ingredients needed to make Avaraikai Poriyal Broad Beans Fry:
  1. Prepare 1/4 kg Avaraikai/ Broad Beans
  2. Get 2 tsp Oil
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp Mustard seeds
  4. Take 1/4 tsp Urad Dal
  5. Take 1 Medium Size Onion chopped
  6. Take Curry leaves few
  7. Get 1 Dry Red Chili broken
  8. Take 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  9. Get 5 Garlic pods roughly chopped
  10. Get Salt-as per your taste
  11. Take 1 tsp Sambar powder
  12. Take Water-as required
  13. Take 1 Tbsp Grated coconut (optional)

Instructions to make Avaraikai Poriyal Broad Beans Fry:
  1. Wash and cut the beans into thin slices as shown in the picture.
  2. In a kadai, add oil. Once oil gets heat, add mustard seeds and urad dal. Fry it.
  3. Add onions, curry leaves, red chilli and turmeric powder. Saute in medium flame till onions becomes translucent.
  4. Add beans, garlic, salt and sambar powder. Mix well.
  5. Add water. Cover and cook till the beans gets cooked. Atlast add grated coconut and mix well. Transfer to a bowl.
  6. Now it's ready to serve. Serve as a Sidedish for your lunch.
