The ingredients needed to make Potato-Broad Beans Sabzi (Aloo sim ki sabji):
  1. Make ready 3 Potatoes
  2. Prepare 250 gms broad beans
  3. Take 1 tomato
  4. Get 2 tsp mustard oil
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  6. Take 1 pinch asafoetida
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 1/2 tsp mirch powder
  9. Make ready 1/3 tsp haldi powder
  10. Make ready 1/3 tsp dhaniya powder

Instructions to make Potato-Broad Beans Sabzi (Aloo sim ki sabji):
  1. Cut and wash potatoes and beans. Also cut tomato.
  2. In a kadhai heat oil, add cumin seeds and asafoetida. (Cooking food in iron kadhai is healthy as it adds iron in food).
  3. Now transfer the broad beans and potatoes to the kadai.
  4. Add all the spices and the tomato pieces in kadai. Mix all the ingredients and cover it and cooks for 15 to 20 minutes on low flame. If required add little bit water.
  5. Once sabji is ready, serve it with paratha or roti.
