The ingredients needed to make Mango Firni:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp basmati rice
  2. Make ready 1 liter ful fat milk
  3. Make ready to taste sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup mango pulp
  5. Prepare 1 tsp cardamom powder
  6. Get 1 tbsp chopped mix dryfruits
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp ghee

Steps to make Mango Firni:
  1. Firstly we wash basmati rice,drain and roast in pan with ghee, grind coursely in mixcy
  2. Heat a large kadai boil milk keep the flame low,or till milk thicken add sugar stir continuously.add grinded mixture,and stir wellmean while we roast chopped dryfruits with ghee till it turns brown colour
  3. Add cardamom powder, chopped dryfruits mix well firni is creamy and thick let it cool
  4. Now add mango pulp and mix well serve chilled and garnished with chopped nuts and some mango pieces
