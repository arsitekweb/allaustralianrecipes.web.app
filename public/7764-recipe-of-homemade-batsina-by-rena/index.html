  2. Take 4 eggs

  4. Get 50 g -100 g butter (we suggest clarified or stakovoutiro -butter that comes from staka, a traditional Cretan dairy product) depending on what you like
  5. Prepare self-rising flour
  6. Make ready fresh milk
  7. Make ready salt & pepper

  3. Heat the butter lightly and add to the mixture.
