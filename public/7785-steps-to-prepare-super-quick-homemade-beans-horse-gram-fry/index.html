The ingredients needed to make Beans Horse Gram Fry:
  1. Make ready 1 . Beans - 100gm
  2. Get 2 . Salt - pinch
  3. Get 3 . Turmeric - pinch
  4. Make ready 4 . Onion - 1 finely chopped
  5. Get 5 . Mustard - 1/2 tsp
  6. Make ready 6 . Urad dal - 1/2 ts
  7. Make ready 7 . Oil - 1 tbsp
  8. Get To Grind:
  9. Get 1 . Horsegram - 1/4 cup (Soak in hot water for 30 minutes or soak overnight)
  10. Make ready 2 . Cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp
  11. Prepare 3 . Red chilli - 3
  12. Make ready 4 . Grated coconut - 2 tbsp

Instructions to make Beans Horse Gram Fry:
    1. Cut the green beans into fine pieces add turmeric and salt. Mix it. Steam this using idli cooker or steamer until done 80%. Keep this aside. - - 2. Soak horse gram for 30 minutes in hot water or soak for overnight in cold water. Drain the water and grind to a coarse paste with cumin, red chilli and coconut.
    1. Heat oil in kadai add mustard and urad dal. Once done add chopped onion to this. - - 4. Now add steamed beans and ground paste. Saute nicely that each beans should coat with horse gram paste. But careful don't break beans.
    1. Adjust salt and serve with sambar rice or you can have with roti as well.
