The ingredients needed to make Milk upsaar:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup horse gram
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup black chickpea
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup black eyed beans
  4. Make ready For masala:
  5. Take 15 green chillies or red chillies
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon jeera
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon pepper
  8. Take 1 whole garlic
  9. Take Handful coriander leaf
  10. Get leaves Few curry
  11. Prepare 1 small lemon size tamarind
  12. Take 1 teaspoon oil
  13. Prepare For Sambhar
  14. Prepare 1 cup milk
  15. Prepare to taste Salt
  16. Prepare For husli:
  17. Get 1 onion diced
  18. Take 3 green chillies
  19. Make ready 2 red chillies
  20. Take Few curry leaves
  21. Get Handful coriander leaves
  22. Get 2 tablespoons fresh grated coconut
  23. Take 1 teaspoon mustard and jeera
  24. Take 1 tablespoon oil
Instructions to make Milk upsaar:
  1. Pressure cook all grains with salt for 4 whistles. Chickpeas should be soaked before cooking. After cooking strain water and keep.
  2. Fry chillies with oil for 5 mins.
  3. Burn the tamarind in flame for 1 min. And grind all ingredients under masala with salt to a smooth paste like chutney don't add more water.
  4. This masala can be used as chutney with roti or can mix with rice, ghee and have. Can be stored for a week with out refrigeration.
  5. Transfer masala to a box. Take 1 tablespoon of it grind with 1 tablespoon of cooked grains to a smooth paste. Add to the water strained from cooked grains. Check taste add salt, tamarind juice and masala according to your taste.
  6. Boil the mixture till raw smell goes. Switch off the stove. After it cools little add milk.
  7. In a pan heat oil add mustard and jeera. After mustard start popping add chillies and curry leaves. Add onions after onions are done add coconut. Add grains mix well add salt if required. Note:already added for cooking grains.
  8. Add chopped coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot with rice.
