The ingredients needed to make Sprouts curry:
  1. Take 1 cup dry horse gram beans
  2. Make ready 1 tsp oil
  3. Prepare Pinch Asafoetida
  4. Prepare Half tsp turmeric powder
  5. Make ready 1 tsp coriander powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
  7. Get 1 tsp garam Masala
  8. Take 10.12 curry leaves
  9. Prepare 1 tsp garlic &ginger paste
  10. Take to taste salt
  11. Prepare 1 chopped onions
  12. Prepare 2 chopped tomatoes
  13. Prepare 1 tsp cumin seeds

Steps to make Sprouts curry:
  1. Wash the horsegram and soak it in water, just like you soak beans or chick peas. Soak overnight or for 10-12 hours.home made grow
  2. Drain and put in a colander for 4-5 hours until most of the water drains out but they are still slightly moist. - - Wrap in a muslin cloth and place on top of an inverted colander. Keep some weight on top of this cloth bundle, such as a wooden cutting board, heavy iron pan etc. - - The beans will sprout in 10-12 hours depending on the ambient temperature.
  3. Pressure cook oil add cumin seeds curry leaves
  4. Now add chopped onions &chopped tomatoes saute few minutes
  5. Now add garlic &ginger paste saute few minutes add red chilli powder turmeric powder coriander powder red garma Masala salt
  6. Mix well saute few minutes now add half cup water 2 whistles and simmer for 10 minutes now sprouts curry ready…
