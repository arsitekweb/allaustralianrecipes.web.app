The ingredients needed to make Basque sausage, goats cheese, broad bean and spring onion salad:
  1. Prepare 1 basque sausage (you could use chorizo also)
  2. Get 100 g goats cheese
  3. Get 12 shells of broad beans
  4. Prepare 1 bunch spring onion
  5. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  6. Get Handful tomatoes
  7. Prepare 1 head lettuce
  8. Make ready Dressing / vinegarette
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  11. Take 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  12. Prepare Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Basque sausage, goats cheese, broad bean and spring onion salad:
  1. Chop the spring onion, garlic and sausage. Shell the beans
  2. In a hot pan add the sausage until the oils start to release then add the beans, spring onions and garlic. Sauté
  3. Mix the dressing ingredients
  4. Chop and wash the tomatoes and salad
  5. Dress the salad with the vinaigrette and top with the beans, sausage and goats cheese 😋
