The ingredients needed to make Persian dill & broad beans rice cake ته چین شوید باقالی:
  1. Prepare 3 cups Iranian rice (due to it’s amazing aroma and taste)
  2. Make ready 400 g chicken fillet, cooked and diced
  3. Take 2 cups Broad beans or Fava beans, cooked
  4. Take 1 cup dried dill
  5. Make ready 500 g Greek yogurt
  6. Prepare 3 eggs
  7. Get 2 onions, finely chopped

  9. Get 3 tbsp Persian saffron water
  10. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp dried rose petals
  12. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  13. Get Vegetable oil
