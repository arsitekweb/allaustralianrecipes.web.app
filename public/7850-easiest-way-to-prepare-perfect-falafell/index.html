The ingredients needed to make Falafell:

  2. Take 1 cup split fava beans
  3. Make ready 1 cup chopped cilantro(coriander)
  4. Take 2 garlic cloves
  5. Make ready 1 small onion diced
  6. Get 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon ground coriander seed
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  9. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt

Steps to make Falafell:
  1. Soak chickpeas and fava beans in water overnight
  2. Start by draining the fava beans & chickpeas well, and grind in the food processor.
  3. Next grind the cilantro, onion, garlic, & add to the ground mixture of chickpeas & fava beans.
  4. Add all the spices and baking powder, and mix the very well.
  5. 10 minutes prior to frying falafel add the baking soda, and mix well.
  6. Using a special falafel mold start scooping the falafel mix into appropriate size tablets as needed
  7. Meanwhile in a large skillet, heat a sufficient amount of vegetable oil for deep frying.
  8. Then start immersing the falafel scoops in the hot oil.
  9. Flip over a couple of times until both sides are golden brown and crunchy on the outside.
  10. Remove from oil and place in a strainer to remove any excess oil.
