The ingredients needed to make Broad beans thuvaiyal:
  1. Take 250 gms Broad beans
  2. Get 15 nos Small onion
  3. Make ready 3 Green chillies
  4. Get Handful Curry leaves
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
  6. Get 2 tablespoons Oil
  7. Take 1 tablespoon Coriander seeds
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds
  9. Get 1 tablespoon Pepper
  10. Make ready 1 tablespoon Split bengal grams
  11. Get 1/4 cup Coconut
  12. Take As per taste Salt
  13. Take As per taste Water

Instructions to make Broad beans thuvaiyal:
  1. Heat a pan add coriander seed, cumin seed, pepper seed, split bengal grams dry roast it. Add 1/4 cup of coconut.
  2. Make a rough powder of roasted spices.
  3. Keep the rough powder aside.
  4. Heat a pan add 1tablespoon of oil add mustard seed, chopped onion, green chilli, curry leaves and fry it till golden brown colour.
  5. Add chopped broad beans add Salt, 1/2 cup of water and cook for 5mins.
  6. Add rough spice powder in that pan and mix it well.
  7. Now thùvaiyal is ready.
