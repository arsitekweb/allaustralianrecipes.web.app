The ingredients needed to make Warm fava bean salad - foul mdammas:
  1. Take 2 cups canned fava beans
  2. Get 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  3. Get 8 tablespoons lemon juice
  4. Take 1/2 cup olive oil
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Take - For garnishing:
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  8. Take 1/2 bunch parsley, finely chopped

This vegetarian dish makes a great snack, appetizer, or a healthy lunch or dinner. It's delicious with toasted and non-toasted pita bread or even on it's own. How to make foul mudammas: Step-by-step. Heat, mash and season fava beans.

Instructions to make Warm fava bean salad - foul mdammas:
  1. Drain and wash the beans, then heat them in a large pan with some water.
  2. Once hot, transfer the beans along with the water into a medium bowl and mash slightly with a fork.
  3. In another bowl, mix the garlic, lemon juice and olive oil with salt and cumin. Pour the dressing over the fava beans and mix well.
  4. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
  5. Serve warm with pita bread, tomato slices and onion wedges.

Remember, you are starting with canned or already cooked fava beans. Add fava beans and juice from the can to a small pot and heat on medium low.
