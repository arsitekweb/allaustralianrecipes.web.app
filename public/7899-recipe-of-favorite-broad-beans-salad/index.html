The ingredients needed to make Broad beans salad:
  1. Get 1 can broad beans
  2. Prepare 2/3 cup yogurt
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp tahini sauce
  4. Prepare 1 tomato chopped
  5. Get 2 lettuce chopped (optional)
  6. Make ready 1 clove garlic minced
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  8. Take pinch salt
  9. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  10. Get 1 tsp ground cumin
  11. Make ready 2 tsp lemon juice

Instructions to make Broad beans salad:
  1. In a bowl mix well yogurt, tahini sauce, lemon juice, garlic and salt
  2. Pour broad beans into a bowl and microwave it for 1 minute then drain it and pour into yogurt sauce
  3. Add the chopped tomato and chopped lettuce to the mixture and fold
  4. Pour the salad into a plate then add olive oil, ground cumin and chopped parsley on the top
