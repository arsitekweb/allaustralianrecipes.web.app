The ingredients needed to make Vegan burger:
  1. Make ready 2 carrots blitzed
  2. Prepare 1 tin chickpeas blitzed
  3. Prepare 10 mushrooms blitzed
  4. Make ready 3 cloves garlic blitzed
  5. Prepare 2 slices breadcrumbs
  6. Get 1 parsnip blitzed
  7. Make ready 150 g flour
  8. Make ready 2 tablespoons mustard
  9. Make ready Teaspoon dried mixed herbs

Instructions to make Vegan burger:
  1. Blitz all of the ingredients except for the flour and mix together in a bowl.
  2. Add the flour
  3. Add oil to a pan and heat. Scoop up mixture with two tablespoons and place gently in the pan and squash down into a burger shape. Turn after 3 minutes.
