The ingredients needed to make Falafel / fava bean falafel:
  1. Prepare 250 grams fava beans
  2. Take 2 cloves garlic
  3. Prepare 1 small onion, quartered
  4. Take 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  5. Make ready 3 tablespoon chopped parsley and cilantro
  6. Make ready to taste Salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  8. Get as needed Neutral oil, like corn, for frying
  9. Get 1/2 tsp Baking powder
