  1. Prepare 1 (3 kg) Milk
  2. Get 2-1 1/4 mutthi Rice (hand ful) soaked for an hour
  3. Make ready 3-1 cup Comelle. If you don't have use sugar according to your sweetness
  4. Take 4-4 Elaichi
  5. Take 5-1 t.sp Kwera
