Instructions to make Mixed Beans Hummus:
  1. Soak the beans overnight and then pressure cook them for 10 minutes on a low flame.
  2. Blend the boiled beans along with roasted sesame seeds, green chili (optional), cumin powder, coriander powder and garlic to a smooth paste.
  3. To the ground paste - add chopped coriander leaves, salt to taste, pepper powder (optional), lime juice, yogurt and olive oil. Mix well.
  4. Serve as a dip, garnished with boiled beans, coriander leaves, olive oil and a pinch of red chili powder (optional).
