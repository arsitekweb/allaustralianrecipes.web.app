The ingredients needed to make Orzo pasta with spinach, Broad beans and feta:
  1. Make ready 1 garlic clove
  2. Take 300 g Greek youghurt
  3. Take 4 tablespoons olive oil
  4. Make ready 1 lemon
  5. Prepare 250 g orzo pasta
  6. Prepare 300 g frozen broad beans
  7. Make ready 150 g asparagus
  8. Take 100 g baby leaf spinach
  9. Take 1 handful fresh basil
  10. Make ready 200 g feta

Steps to make Orzo pasta with spinach, Broad beans and feta:
  1. Place a large bowl of salted water on the hob and bring to the boil. While the water is coming to the boil add the minced garlic, youghurt, oil, lemon juice and salt and pepper in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Add the orzo to the boil water and cook for 10-12minutes. Minutes before the pasta is cooked add the frozen beans and asparagus.
  3. Take a cup of the past water and set aside for later Allowing to cool slightly. Drain the pasta and veg.
  4. In a bowl mix the yoghurt, pasta, beans and asparagus. Slowly add some of the pasta water, mixing thoroughly adding bit by bit until the water has been absorbed.
  5. To serve top the pasta mixture with torn spinach and basil leaves, feta and a drizzle of olive oil.
