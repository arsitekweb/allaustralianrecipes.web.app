The ingredients needed to make Fall veggie delight:
  1. Prepare 2 cup Cubed carrots
  2. Get 2 cup Cubed butternut squash
  3. Get 2 cup Coined parsnips
  4. Prepare 2 clove Minced garlic
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup Diced onion
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Diced celery
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Dill
  8. Take 1 tsp ground thyme
  9. Prepare Salt and pepper
  10. Take 7 oz bite size bacon pieces (optional)
  11. Make ready 7 oz spinach (optional..drain well if not fresh)

Instructions to make Fall veggie delight:
  1. In a large saucepan put your carrots squash and parsnips in enough water to cover boil until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile sauté in butter your celery onion and garlic in a large pan about 5-10 minutes on medium heat.
  3. When vegetables are al dente, drain and mix in pan with celery onion garlic mixture.
  4. Season vegetable mixture with dill, thyme, and salt and pepper…serve :)
  5. Try adding (which I do) 6-8 ounces of bite size bacon piece and spinach to sauté mixture before adding boiled vegetables.
