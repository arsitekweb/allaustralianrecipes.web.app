#stayingathome:

  1. Get 1 can Fava beans
  2. Take to taste Salt
  3. Prepare 1 tsp olive oil
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp tomato paste
  5. Get 1/4 of turmeric powder
  6. Get 1/4 of chilli powder
  7. Prepare 2 tsp Fresh lime juice
  8. Take Fresh coriander hand full
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp Fried onion (optional)
