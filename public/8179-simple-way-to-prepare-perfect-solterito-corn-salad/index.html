The ingredients needed to make Solterito | Corn Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 cup "choclo" (yellow or white corn), threshed
  2. Prepare 1 cup broad beans
  3. Take 1 cup lima beans
  4. Get 1 cup tomatoes, diced
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup red onion, chopped
  6. Make ready 1 pound "queso fresco" (white cheese), diced
  7. Get 1/2 cup black or green olives, sliced crosswise
  8. Take 1/3 cup parsley, chopped
  9. Get 2 teaspoons "aji limo" (lime chilli), minced w/o seeds or veins
  10. Take 5 teaspoons olive oil
  11. Take 3 teaspoons red vinegar
  12. Take 5 lettuce leaves
  13. Get to taste Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Solterito | Corn Salad:
  1. Place the corn kernels, broad beans, and lima beans in a pot with salted water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat for about 10mins. or until beans are just tender. Drain them and let them rest.
  2. Gently mix all the ingredients in a bowl (corn kernels, broad beans, lima beans, tomatoes, red onion, white cheese, black olives, parsley lime chilli, olive oil, red vinegar, salt and pepper).
  3. Serve each dish over a lettuce, and decorate with some parsley on top.
