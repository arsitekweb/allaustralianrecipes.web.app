The ingredients needed to make Sooji and vermicelli mix halwa:
  1. Prepare 1/2 Cup sooji
  2. Make ready 2 Bowls Sugar or adjust your according to your taste
  3. Make ready 1/2 Cup roasted vermicelli
  4. Get 1 1/2 Bowl Desi Ghee
  5. Take 3 1/2 Cups milk or adjust accordingly
  6. Prepare as required Roasted Nuts (Almonds) and grated coconut
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom powder
  8. Make ready as required Garnishing: colour ful grated coconut, green cardamom
  9. Prepare as required Roasted vermicelli and sooji
