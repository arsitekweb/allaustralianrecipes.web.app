The ingredients needed to make Carrot and coriander soup with a twist:
  1. Take 570 grams carrots ends chopped, peel on optional and sliced
  2. Prepare 181 grams parsnips ends chopped, peel left on optional and sliced
  3. Take 95 grams peeled and cubed potatoes
  4. Make ready 1 vegetable stock cube
  5. Make ready 900 ml Cold Water
  6. Get 1 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  7. Make ready 1 tsp asafoetida
