The ingredients needed to make Urap Bali

#eattherainbow:

  1. Prepare 300 gr fresh shredded coconut (if frozen, thaw it completely)
  2. Get 2 long red chillies (more if you like)
  3. Make ready 4 eshallots or 1 red onion
  4. Prepare 4 garlic
  5. Take 1 cm galangal
  6. Get 1/2 cm lesser galangal
  7. Make ready 5 kaffir lime leaves (remove the middle stem)
  8. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  9. Make ready 1 TBSP coconut sugar
  10. Get 1 TBSP oil
  11. Get 1 cup shredded carrots
  12. Take 1.5 cup shredded silver beets
  13. Make ready 1 cup bean sprouts (remove tails)
  14. Prepare 4 pcs snake beans chopped
  15. Make ready 1 zucchini julienne cut

Instructions to make Urap Bali

#eattherainbow:

  1. Dry fry coconut until a little bit brown. Set aside.
  2. Blend all spice. Fry with oil until fragrant.
  3. Pour the shredded coconut in the same pan. Stir until all mixed. It should be a bit too salty at this stage.
  4. Steam veggies until cooked. Snake bean takes longer to cook.
  5. Mix all veggies with the spices coconut. Serve.
