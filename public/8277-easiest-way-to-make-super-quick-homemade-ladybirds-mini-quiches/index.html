The ingredients needed to make Ladybirds Mini Quiches .:
  1. Get 1 batch short crust pastry .
  2. Get 2 eggs - whisked .
  3. Take 3 tbsp milk .
  4. Prepare 3 cherry tomatoes .
  5. Prepare 1 few baby silverbeet leaves - chopped into big pieces .
  6. Get 1 medium piece capsicum - de-seeded and finely diced .
  7. Get 1 half dozen basil leaves - chopped .
  8. Make ready 1 half dozen oregano leaves - chopped .
  9. Make ready 1 few sprigs of dill - torn up .
  10. Take 1 few sprigs parsley - torn up .
  11. Prepare 1 salt
  12. Prepare 1 cracked pepper .

Instructions to make Ladybirds Mini Quiches .:
  1. prepare shortcrust pastry and set in fridge till needed . or use premade short crust pastry .
  2. preheat oven to 180¬∞C / 360¬∞F . grease or foil line a couple patty tins and set aside till needed .
  3. either roll out pastry to about 2cm thickness and cut out pastry circles to fit prepared patty tins to create pastry shells , i used an egg ring for size to fit tins , and prick inside bottom of pastry shells with a fork to prevent it from rising . lightly egg wash pastry shells inside and place in heated oven for 12-15 minutes or till lightly golden , remove from heat and let cool in trays for couple minutes then remove from trays or foil cases to cool completely on a wire rack .
  4. in a bowl whisk the eggs then add the milk and whisk them together then pour evenly into prepared pastry shells to 3/4 full . add a tomato slice then a few bits of silver beet then some of the capsicum and a little of the basil and oregano or basil and dill then sprinkle some feta cheese in it followed by little parsley and finished with pinch of salt and cracked pepper .
  5. place prepared little quiches in preheated over for 12-15 minutes or till egg mix is set . garnish with parsley and serve hot . enjoy . :-) .
