The ingredients needed to make Amy's Spinach and Feta Cheese Omelette .:
  1. Take 4 eggs
  2. Take 1/3 cup milk
  3. Prepare 1 dozen baby silverbeet leaves
  4. Prepare 3 lemon sage leaves
  5. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  6. Make ready 1 ground cracked black pepper
  7. Prepare 1 a little oil for cooling .
  8. Make ready 1 sprig lemon sage for garnish .

Instructions to make Amy's Spinach and Feta Cheese Omelette .:
  1. place eggs into a bowl and whisk , add the milk and whisk well now add pinch salt and some ground cracked black pepper , mix well to combine all
  2. wash and slice silverbeet and lemon sage into thin strips , brake feta Cheese into small pieces .
  3. place a little oil in the bottom of a frypan to coat the base lightly and heat .
  4. add the egg mix to the heated oil then sprinkle the silverbeet , sage and broken up feta into the egg mix making sure all is immersed in the egg mix . turn heat to medium low and cook for 3-5 minutes until bottom egg mix is set and top starts to set .
  5. place under a hot grill to set and brown top a little . remove from grill and serve Garnished with a sprig of lemon sage , serve hot . enjoy . :-) .
