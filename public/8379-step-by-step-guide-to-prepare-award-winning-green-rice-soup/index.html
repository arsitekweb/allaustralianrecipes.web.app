The ingredients needed to make Green Rice Soup:
  1. Take 1 & 1/2 cups Dashi Stock OR Chicken Stock
  2. Prepare *Note: 1 & 1/2 cups Water and 3/4 teaspoon Dashi Powder
  3. Take 1 cup Cooked Short Grain Rice *firmly packed
  4. Take 1 Spring Onion *finely chopped, save some for topping
  5. Get 1 cupful chopped Green Vegetables *e.g. Bok Choy, Spinach, Silverbeet Leaves, Kale, etc
  6. Take 1/4 cup Edamame (Young Soy Beans)
  7. Take 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  8. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon Mirin
  9. Make ready Salt as required
  10. Get 1/2 teaspoon Grated Ginger
  11. Make ready 1/2-1 sheet Toasted Nori (Seaweed Sheet) *torn into small pieces

Steps to make Green Rice Soup:
  1. Place Dashi Stock and Cooked Rice in a saucepan, bring to the boil over medium high heat. Break down any lumps.
  2. Add all Vegetables, bring to the boil, reduce heat to low and simmer until vegetables are cooked. Season with Soy Sauce, Mirin and Salt.
  3. Add grated Ginger and stir, then remove from the heat.
  4. Pour the rice soup in a serving bowl, place plenty of Toasted Nori and saved Spring Onion on top, and enjoy.
