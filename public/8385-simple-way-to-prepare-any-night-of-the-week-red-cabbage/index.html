The ingredients needed to make Red cabbage:
  1. Take 200 g red cabbage
  2. Prepare 500 ml water
  3. Take 17 g salt

Steps to make Red cabbage:
  1. Slice cabbage finely and soak in cold water to wash
  2. Pack jar with cabbage and top it up with water
  3. Add the weight of water plus cabbage and calculate 2.5% of the weight which will be the amount of salt needed to make the brine.
  4. Strain the water, Dissolve the salt in the water and return to the container with the cabbage.
  5. Leave at room temperature making sure cabbage is completely submerged to create the conditions for lactofermentation. Keep top loose to allow gas to scape
  6. After 10 days keep jar in fridge. Enjoy
