The ingredients needed to make Simple and delicious red cabbage and green bean stir fry:
  1. Make ready 1/2 medium sized onion thinly sliced
  2. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 500 g red cabbage
  5. Prepare 1 1/3 cup green beans
  6. Get 2 tsp ginger powder
  7. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Maggi liquid seasoning
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp soy sauce

Steps to make Simple and delicious red cabbage and green bean stir fry:
  1. Put some oil in a pan and add the onions and brown sugar then caramelise until golden brown. Then add the salt.
  2. Add in the red cabbage and green beans along with the ginger powder. Then the Maggi liquid seasoning and soy sauce. Cook until soft on the outside but still crunchy inside
