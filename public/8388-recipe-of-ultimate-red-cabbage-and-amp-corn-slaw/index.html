The ingredients needed to make Red Cabbage & Corn Slaw:
  1. Take 300 g Red Cabbage *1/6 of large head
  2. Get 1 Carrot
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  4. Take 1/2 cup Corn Kernels *Frozen Kernels are fine
  5. Make ready Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  6. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon Sugar
  7. Get 4 tablespoons Japanese Mayonnaise
  8. Make ready 1 & 1/2 tablespoons Rice Vinegar
