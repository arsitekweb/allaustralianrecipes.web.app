The ingredients needed to make Red Cabbage Sloppy Rice:
  1. Get 2 tablespoons Butter OR Olive Oil
  2. Get 1 clove Garlic *finely chopped
  3. Take 1/2 Onion *finely chopped
  4. Take 3-4 leaves Red Cabbage *about 200g, cut into small pieces
  5. Get 1 cup (*180ml cup) Arborio Rice
  6. Prepare 500 ml Chicken Stock OR Vegetable Stock
  7. Make ready Salt & Pepper
  8. Get 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan *plus extra for serving
  9. Prepare Pistachio Nuts *optional

Instructions to make Red Cabbage Sloppy Rice:
  1. Heat Butter (OR Olive Oil) in a saucepan over medium low heat, cook Garlic and Onion for a few minutes, add Red Cabbage and cook until slightly softened. Add Arborio Rice and combine.
  2. Place the mixture into the rice cooker’s inner pot. Add Chicken Stock (OR Water and crumbled Stock Cube). Season with Salt & Pepper as required. *Note: If the Stock is salted, you only need a small amount or no Salt.
  3. Press ‘COOK’ button to start cooking. If your rice cooker has ‘Quit Cook’ setting, use it.
  4. When the rice is cooked, add Parmesan and mix well. Sprinkle with some extra grated Parmesan and finely chopped Pistachio Nuts, and enjoy.
