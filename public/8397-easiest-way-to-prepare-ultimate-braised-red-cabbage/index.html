Instructions to make Braised red cabbage:
  1. Turn on oven to 180.
  2. Chop cabbage. Place a large casserole dish.
  3. Add balsamic. Stir.
  4. Add salt. Stir.
  5. Add water. Stir.
  6. Put into oven for 30 mins. Then reduce heat to 100 degrees and cook for an hour or longer. It will sit braising away nicely for several hours if you wish.
  7. Eat!
