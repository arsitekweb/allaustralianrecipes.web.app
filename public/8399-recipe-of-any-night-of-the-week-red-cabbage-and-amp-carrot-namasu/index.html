The ingredients needed to make Red Cabbage & Carrot ‘Namasu’:
  1. Prepare 1/6 Red Cabbage *about 200g
  2. Take 1 Carrot *about 100g
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  4. Take 2 tablespoons Rice Vinegar
  5. Take 1 tablespoon Sugar
  6. Take Toasted Sesame Seeds

Steps to make Red Cabbage & Carrot ‘Namasu’:
  1. Thinly slice Red Cabbage.
  2. Peel and slice Carrot into 2 to 3mm thin strips. I usually use a slicer. If you cut by knife, slice them diagonally 2 to 3mm thin, then cut into 2 to 3mm strips.
  3. Mix Vinegar, Sugar and Salt, add vegetables and mix well. Set aside for about ten minutes to let it well seasoned. Sprinkle some Toasted Sesame Seeds on top and serve.
