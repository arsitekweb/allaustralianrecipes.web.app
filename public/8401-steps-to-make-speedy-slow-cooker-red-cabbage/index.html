The ingredients needed to make Slow cooker red cabbage:
  1. Prepare 1 Red cabbage
  2. Prepare 1 small onion
  3. Get 2 green apples
  4. Get 2 tbsp cider vinegar
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Get 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  8. Get to taste salt & pepper
  9. Get cooking spray

Steps to make Slow cooker red cabbage:
  1. Spray pot inner
  2. Quarter cabbage, remove core, shred finely, place in pot
  3. Peel and finely slice onion, add to pot.
  4. Peel, core and finely slice apples, add to pot.
  5. Add seasonings and sugar, stir, mixing well.
  6. Add cider vinegar and 2 tablespoons water.
  7. Switch slowcooker to low, cover with lid and cook for 4 hours, stirring occasionally
  8. Serve.
  9. You can add a handful of raisins or dried cranberries before cooking.
  10. A tablespoon of balsamic vinegar added just before serving adds a richer taste.
  11. You can substitute red wine or red wine vinegar for cider vinegar
