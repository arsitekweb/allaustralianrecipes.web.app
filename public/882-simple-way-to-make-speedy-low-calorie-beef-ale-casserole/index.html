The ingredients needed to make Low Calorie Beef Ale Casserole:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
  2. Prepare 2 medium onions chopped
  3. Prepare 1 kg braising beef cubed
  4. Make ready to taste salt pepper
  5. Prepare 500 ml dark ale (beer)
  6. Get 250 ml beef stock made with stock cube
  7. Take 2 tbsp tomato paste
  8. Make ready to taste mixed herbs
  9. Prepare 6 carrots chopped
  10. Take 2 parsnips or any other root veg you want
  11. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  12. Prepare 1 small amount of sugar to taste
  13. Make ready 10 mushrooms chopped up

Steps to make Low Calorie Beef Ale Casserole:
  1. Chop cook onions in little oil and add to casserole dish
  2. Cube and brown beef in same dish as onions, season with salt and pepper
  3. Add all the other ingredients to casserole pot and cook low for three to four hours till thick and nice.
  4. Enjoy with mash or roasted potatoes
  5. Can be cooked in slow cooker or oven or on stove top
