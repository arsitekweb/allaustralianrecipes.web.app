The ingredients needed to make Spinach Soup:
  1. Take 6 cup spinach
  2. Prepare 1 can parsnip greens
  3. Prepare 1 can fire roasted tomatoes
  4. Prepare 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  5. Prepare 1 can white baby potatoes
  6. Take 1 cup lentils, cooked
  7. Make ready 1 cup tofu, cubed
  8. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  9. Make ready 6 tbsp garlic, minced
  10. Prepare 1 lemon, juiced
  11. Take 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
